# Solar System

The solar system which Mazariim is a part has four planets, and the distance from the sun is measured in Astronomical Units, with 1 AU = the distance from Mazariim to the sun.

## Dicia

This small rocky planet speeds around the sun at just .29 AU, with a year comprised of just 92 days. Incredibly hot and barren, no known life has ever developed on Dicia. It would be comparable to Mercury in our real-world solar system.

## Mazariim

Perfectly situated in the habitable zone of its solar system, Mazariim is the main place life has developed and flourished. Mazariim has two moons, Io'el and Randir'el, meaning "Night Star" and "Wandering Star" in elven. One keeps a traditional lunar cycle of 28 days and is in line with the calendars of the world, with full moons signifying the beginning of a new month. The other has an irregular orbit, swinging to further extremes and appearing larger and smaller throughout its seven-month orbit.

## Orpheous

This giant planet made of gas is around 10 AU from its sun and is very much like the planet Saturn in our real-world solar system. Orpheous is so large that you can see its rings on a very clear and cloudless night. It has several moons of various types and sizes.

## Intiago

Further out still at around 17 AU, Intiago is a huge gas giant very much like Neptune of our real-world solar system. Intiago has many moons as well, including one named Lystra. This moon enjoyed a habitable surface for millions of years because of interior vulcanism and a rampant greenhouse effect. It was there that the progenitors of dragons originated, eventually fleeing their world to escape its irradiation.
