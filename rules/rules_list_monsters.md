# Cypher System Creature Compendium - GM Quick Reference

## Conversion Rules Summary

- **Creature Level**: Based on HD (see HD→Level table below)
- **Health**: Level × size multiplier (Small=3, Medium=4, Large=5, Huge+=6), +1-2/lv for high Might
- **Stat Mods**: Stats 1-4 = -2 lv; 5-8 = -1 lv; 9-13 = 0; 14-21 = +1 lv; 22-30 = +2 lv
- **Armor**: AC 10-12=0; 13-15=1; 16-19=2; 20-23=3; +1 per additional 4 AC; Shield=Asset Speed def
- **Size → Speed Def**: Small +1; Medium 0; Large -1; Huge+ -2
- **Extra-Planar**: +1 level; **Dragon**: +2 level
- **Effort (monsters)**: 1 Effort/level; Lv1-3: 1 at once (1-3×); Lv4-6: up to 2 at once (2-5×); Lv7-10: up to 3 at once (3-10×)
- **Modification Caps**: Low-tier ≤2 mods; Mid-tier ≤4 mods; High-tier 5+

### NPC Spellcaster Quick Build
1. Pick a Focus → gives 6 spells
2. All spellcasters get Magical Training free
3. NPC Potency: assume up to half their level
4. NPC Effort resistance: assume up to half their level

## Conditions Quick Reference

| Condition | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **Blinded** | Auto-fail sight tasks; attacks & defense hindered 2 steps |
| **Charmed** | Can't attack charmer; social defense vs charmer hindered |
| **Deafened** | Auto-fail hearing tasks |
| **Frightened** | Attacks & skills hindered while source visible; can't approach source |
| **Grappled** | Speed 0; ends if grappler incapacitated |
| **Incapacitated** | Can't take actions |
| **Invisible** | Attacks & defense against it hindered 2 steps |
| **Paralyzed** | Incapacitated; can't move/speak; auto-fail Might/Speed; attacks eased 2 steps; melee hits = crits |
| **Petrified** | Incapacitated; immune poison/disease; attacks eased 2 steps; auto-fail Might/Speed |
| **Poisoned** | Hindered on all tasks |
| **Prone** | Hindered attacks; melee vs it hindered, ranged vs it eased |
| **Restrained** | Speed 0; attacks & defense hindered; no somatic spells; light weapons only |
| **Stunned** | Incapacitated; can't move; speak falteringly; auto-fail Might/Speed; defense hindered |
| **Unconscious** | Incapacitated; drops items; falls prone; auto-fail Might/Speed; melee hits = crits |
