# Cypher System Spell Compendium - GM Quick Reference

## Spell Rules Summary

- **Spell Attacks**: Always Intellect-based (even if defense is Might/Speed)
- **Intellect Cost**: Generally original D&D level +1 (or +2 for high tier)
- **Effort on Damage**: +3 single-target, +2 area, +1 sustained/repeating
- **Spell Damage Formula**: Single-target = level +3; Area = level +2
- **Potency**: Effort spent to make spells harder to dispel
- **Spamming**: Only one instance of a spell active at once (unless noted)
- **Armor**: Spell damage reduced by Armor unless noted; "ignores Armor" = ignores normal Armor; Elemental Armor always applies vs energy
- **Defensive Casting**: Level 4 Spellcraft task (if using AoO rules)
- **Concentration Check**: Spellcraft vs level = damage taken
- **Magical Training** (free): Trained in magic/Spellcraft; spells need verbal (Draconic) + somatic; can't cast in untrained armor
- **Counterspell**: Readied action, Intellect attack, eased by Spellcraft ranks, hindered by target's Spellcraft/Effort
- **Half-damage on defense**: Low/mid area spells = 1 dmg (ignores Armor); High tier = half dmg
