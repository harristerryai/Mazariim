# Entity Descriptions

In the universe of Mazariim, the gods do not hold sway over the powers of creation and existence. They themselves are merely sentient beings as well, capable of being banished or even destroyed. These entities, although sentient, would often slumber for millions or even billions of years at a time.

## **Alagalothor** (ah-la-LOW-thor)

**Portfolio**: Magic, spellcasting  
**Power Level**: Greater entity (original universe: Talernon)  
**Title(s)**: Dragon Mage, Archmage of Archmages  
**Dominion**: Astral Plane  
**Worshipers**: Spellcasters, magical creatures  
**Favored Race**: Human  
**Symbol**: Magical tendrils wrapped around a dragon eye  
**Alignment**: N  
**Favored Weapon**: Staff  
**Foci**: Studies Abjuration, Studies Conjuration, Studies Divination, Studies Enchantment, Studies Evocation, Studies Illusion, Studies Necromancy, Studies Transmutation, Worships Arcana  

**Description**: Alagalothor is the patron deity/entity of magic and spellcasters. He embodies magic in all forms, as well as the sense of discovery in creating new magics and spells as well as magical items. Originally from another universe, it was during a titanic battle with the avatar of Sheeva and the time-traveling archmage Thalaman that the trio was ripped from their universe as it destroyed itself and transported to Mazariim in 1492 BW. His clerics are called Arcanologists, and they frequently search for lost magics and share them with others. Always observing how mortals use magic from his seat in the Astral Plane.

## **Ashura**

**Portfolio**: Honor, light, healing  
**Power Level**: Lesser entity  
**Title(s)**: Golden Blade, Most Honorable Mistress  
**Dominion**: Positive Energy Plane (Tengoku)  
**Worshipers**: Healers, kensai, samurai, Ejiin  
**Favored Race**: Human (Ejiin)  
**Symbol**: Crossed katana and wakizashi within healing energies  
**Alignment**: LG  
**Favored Weapon**: Bo staff  
**Foci**: Worships Life, Worships Light, Worships Order, Worships Protection  

**Description**: Ashura is a unique lesser entity venerated by the Ejiin faith, associated with healing and good, served by Kami spirits. An androgynous entity clad in samurai plate, Ashura holds tea ceremonies in Tengoku and battles Khan. Typically defeats adversaries but at great cost. Ashura's clerics emphasize honor, duty, and the healing arts.

## **Asti**

**Portfolio**: Healing, dragons, ley-lines  
**Power Level**: Greater entity (Old God)  
**Title(s)**: Healing Breath, Mistress of Dragons  
**Dominion**: Positive Energy Plane  
**Worshipers**: Healers, dragons, ley-walkers  
**Favored Race**: Human (Akovarians/Varkonans)  
**Symbol**: Pyramid with ley-lines intersecting it  
**Alignment**: LG  
**Favored Weapon**: Whip  
**Foci**: Worships Life, Worships Light, Worships Protection, Worships Arcana  

**Description**: Asti is one of the oldest entities, born of pure energy around a white hole approximately 20,000 years before the Great War. She settled on Mazariim around 12,000 years ago, taking female form and conferring immunity to plagues, faster healing, and longer life to her followers. Patron of the ancient Akovar civilization, her avatar powered the ley-lines—magical rivers that enabled ritual-enhanced spellcasting. During the Dragon War around 6,830 BW, priests transferred her essence into the Mask of the Pharaoh as the ley-lines exploded. Her release is heralded by ley-line resurgence; followers tend toward non-violence.
