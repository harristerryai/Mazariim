# Human Migration Patterns Across Nigal
## The Spread of Humanity Across the Continent

**PURPOSE**: Documents human origins, migration waves, cultural splits, and re-contact dates. Used to maintain consistency in human cultural development and explain regional diversity.

## HUMAN ORIGINS ON MAZARIIM

### **Two Separate Human Populations on Nigal:**

1. **NATIVE NIGAL HUMANS** (Inner Kingdoms - Madronians)
   - Origin: NATIVE to Nigal, emerged naturally in temperate northwest
   - Homeland: Inner Kingdoms / Madronian Peninsula
   - Timeline: Ancient (possibly 10,000+ BW)
   - Named after: Wizard-king Madron of Yrugas

2. **AKOVAR REFUGEES** (Ancient South - Varkonans)
   - Origin: Evacuated from Akovar continent during Dragon War
   - Arrival: ~6,830 BW (magical ritual teleportation)
   - Homeland: Ancient South
   - Named after: "God-king" in their ancient language

## MIGRATION WAVES FROM INNER KINGDOMS (Madronian Origin)

### **WAVE 1: South to Merchant Nations** (~3,500 BW)
**Madronian → Sabonaar Cultural Split**

**Timeline:**
- ~3,500 BW: Migration begins along western coast
- ~3,200 BW: Sabonaar culture distinct from Madronian
- ~1,328 BW: Sabonaa unified as nation

**Route:** Inner Kingdoms → South along western coast → Sabon Peninsula

**Reasons:**
- Population pressure after Yrugas period
- Trade opportunities (coastal resources, fishing)
- Yrugas collapse (~2,700 BW) created refugee waves
- Seeking distance from brutal mageocratic past

**Cultural Changes:**
- Developed maritime traditions
- Mercantile focus (vs Madronian intellectual/artistic)
- Created Sabo language (phonetic, practical)
- Seven noble houses with 2,000+ year lineages

**Result:** Sabonaar people, distinct from Madronians
- Founded: Sabonaa (1,328 BW), later Naarlam (555 BW), Dukhan (1,001 BW), etc.

---

### **WAVE 2: East to Horselands** (~2,500 BW)
**Madronian → Chak'ti Cultural Split**

**Timeline:**
- ~2,500 BW: Migration to central plains begins
- ~2,500 BW: Khonayn sacred site established
- ~400 BW: Sharma-Ad formalized as mobile nation

**Route:** Inner Kingdoms → East through central corridor → Great Plains

**Reasons:**
- Cultural/spiritual calling (ancestor worship)
- Following great horse herds
- Desire to abandon settled agricultural life
- Escaping Yrugas aftermath and conflicts

**Cultural Changes:**
- Abandoned cities for nomadic life
- Developed horse-based culture
- Ancestor worship became central
- Tent-cities and mobile living

**Result:** Chak'ti people
- Founded: Khonayn holy grounds (2,500 BW), Sharma-Ad (400 BW)

---

### **WAVE 3: North to Frozen Isles** (~2,000 BW)
**Madronian → Nond Cultural Split**

**Timeline:**
- ~2,000 BW: Northern exploration begins
- ~1,800 BW: Javalh founded (first settlement)
- ~1,200 BW: Brahjhin founded
- ~350 AW: Nond founded (recent expansion)

**Route:** Inner Kingdoms → North through Gnomish Lands → Far North

**Reasons:**
- Adventure and exploration
- Resources (furs, fishing, timber)
- Escaping southern population pressure
- Hardy peoples seeking challenge

**Cultural Changes:**
- Developed cold-weather survival skills
- Vikingesque seafaring culture
- Honorable warrior traditions
- Raiding and trading mix

**Result:** Nond people
- Founded: Javalh (1,800 BW), Brahjhin (1,200 BW), Nond (350 AW)

---

### **WAVE 4: Yrugas Refugee Crisis** (~2,700 BW)
**Yrugas Collapse → Kruta (NOT typical migration)**

**Timeline:**
- ~2,700 BW: Yrugas catastrophic collapse
- ~2,700-2,650 BW: Surviving mages flee south
- ~2,650 BW: Kruta founded in Lavantine Peninsula (Lands of Chaos)

**Route:** Inner Kingdoms (Yrugas ruins) → South to Lavantine Peninsula

**Reasons:**
- REFUGEE CRISIS, not planned migration
- Magical catastrophe and slave revolts
- Surviving wizard-lords flee for survival
- Seeking isolated area to rebuild power

**Cultural Changes:**
- Mixed with native Laveri peoples
- Maintained brutal Yrugan mageocracy
- Continued slavetrading practices
- Preserved necromantic traditions
- Isolated from other Madronian cultures

**Result:** Kruta slavetrading magocracy (mixed Yrugas/Laveri)
- Founded: Kruta (2,650 BW) - only ~50 years after collapse

## MIGRATION WAVES FROM ANCIENT SOUTH (Varkonan Origin)

### **WAVE 1: East to Lands of Honor** (~3,500 BW)
**Varkonan → Ejiin Cultural Split**

**Timeline:**
- ~3,500 BW: Eastern migration begins
- ~3,400-3,200 BW: Settlement and cultural development
- ~3,200 BW: Miyasaki Shogunate formalized
- ~2,800 BW: Veppankarai Islands settled

**Route:** Ancient South (Nepherites) → East along southern coast → Lands of Honor (Yashaki Islands)

**Reasons:**
- Population pressure in Nepherites/Uadjit
- Seeking new lands for expansion
- Spiritual visions of elemental forces
- Desire to spread beyond desert/river regions

**Cultural Changes:**
- Mixed with possible indigenous peoples (?)
- Abandoned pharaonic traditions
- Developed NEW elemental religion (water, earth, air, fire)
- Shogunate system replaced god-kings
- Lost direct connection to Akovar/ley-line knowledge
