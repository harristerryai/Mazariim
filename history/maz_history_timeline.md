# Mazariim Master Timeline
## Comprehensive Chronological Reference

**PURPOSE**: Single authoritative source for all historical dates in Mazariim. Used to maintain consistency across all worldbuilding documents.

**DATE SYSTEM**: BW (Before War) and AW (After War), with Year 1 AW marking the end of the War to End All Wars.

---

# THE TIMELESS ERA

## Cosmic Origins

**Approximately 13.7 billion BW — The Birth of the Universe**

The universe of Mazariim evolved from ancient cosmic processes—through the merging of primordial reality streams from predecessor dimensions. Matter and energy explode outward into near-limitless expanse.

**Approximately 2 billion BW — Mau the Oracle Awakens**

The first sentient being in the universe emerges from accumulated magical energy. Mau creates the Astral Plane and Astral Sea, establishing infrastructure for souls to transfer between bodies and planes upon death. Mau exists in solitude for roughly 1.5 billion years, billions of years before mortal life emerged around 100,000 BW.

**Approximately 4 billion BW — The Solar System Forms**

Four planets coalesce around a stable yellow star: Dicia (rocky, close to sun), Mazariim (habitable zone), Orpheous (ringed gas giant), and Intiago (massive outer gas giant).

**Approximately 1.5 billion BW — Life Emerges on Mazariim**

Single-celled organisms stir in primordial seas. Over hundreds of millions of years, life evolves increasing complexity.

**~400 million BW — Complex Life on Land**

Life on land becomes diverse and complex, forming intricate ecosystems. Mazariim becomes a living world.

**~2 million BW — The Drakaii Arrive**

On Lystra (moon of Intiago), the advanced Drakaii civilization faces destruction. Survivors scatter across dimensions and universes. A small group arrives on Mazariim and becomes the progenitors of dragons.

**~500,000 BW — Evolution of Mortal Races Begins**

A common ancestor species begins splitting into distinct lineages that will eventually become Elves, Humans, Dwarves, Halflings, and Gnomes.

**~100,000 BW — Genetic Distinction of Major Races**

The five major races become genetically distinct species, no longer capable of interbreeding. Each develops unique cultures and survival strategies.

**~50,000 BW — Stone Tools and Fire**

Stone tools, fire, and rudimentary language become widespread among advancing races. Family-based clans form.

**~25,000 BW — The Old Gods Begin Awakening**

Elemental entities begin awakening across several thousand years, each subsequent awakening nudging others into consciousness:
- **Pyrantor** (Fire/Sun) — from stellar fusion in a distant star
- **Iquinias** (Water/Oceans) — on a distant water world
- **Vuleala** (Air/Weather) — from atmospheric currents across countless worlds
- **Dirgin** (Earth/Caves) — on a barren rocky body
- **Zebulba** (Death/Entropy) — formed within and absorbed a black hole, drawing power from the Negative Energy Plane
- **Gruhathyrir** (Wild Nature) — from the savage wilderness

**~12,000 BW — Asti Awakens**

Unlike other Old Gods, Asti (Healing/Light) forms from the cosmic merger that created the universe—emerging from interactions between realities where incompatible laws of physics sought equilibrium. Asti begins establishing the ley-line network.

**~20,000 BW — The Feywild Forms**

Gruhathyrir's consciousness warps reality, creating the Feywild as a parallel plane where nature operates by dream-logic. First fey creatures emerge: sprites, dryads, satyrs, and archfey lords like Titania and Oberon.

**~20,000 BW — First Death Cults**

Early mortals experimenting with necromancy independently discover Zebulba. First death cults form among humans and dwarves; most are destroyed by their own creations or purged by rivals.

**~18,000 BW — Og'thallith Awakens**

Entity of insanity and demonic corruption merges with the Abyss, establishing a realm of gibbering chaos. Demons begin tempting early spellcasters into dark pacts.

**~17,000 BW — Xorancon Awakens in the Far Realm**

The most alien of entities awakens in spaces between realities. Creates the Far Realm—home of all aberrations—manifesting as a titanic beholder-like entity of impossible geometry.

**~16,000 BW — Kazrokath Awakens**

Entity of change, entropy, and transformation awakens. Creates Limbo—a realm of boundless chaos served by serpentine Proteans.

**~14,200 BW — Elwendor Founded**

On the continent of Vand'ardhon, elves establish Elwendor—the first permanent elven city and cultural heart of elvenkind.

**~13,000 BW — The Akovar Civilization Begins**

On the isolated continent of Akovar, the first truly advanced civilization arises around collaboration with Asti. While other races form small clans, Akovarians construct pyramid temples, develop written language, and master healing magic.

**~12,000 BW — Iaurtorn Consecrated**

Elven druids consecrate Iaurtorn, a vast ceremonial forest on Vand'ardhon serving as neutral ground for all elven clans.

**~12,000 BW — Zanzabar Awakens**

Youngest Timeless Era entity coalesces from dream-energies after sentient races evolve complex dreams. Creates the Plane of Dreams—ever-changing transitive plane through which dreaming minds travel.

**~10,000 BW — Agriculture Expands**

Simple agriculture becomes commonplace. Small settlements grow into villages; competition fuels conflict between races and clans.

**~8,200 BW — Akovarian Pyramids Completed; First Ley-Lines Energized**

Akovarian pyramids at ley-line convergence points are completed. First ley-lines are energized, creating magical infrastructure.

**~8,000 BW — Tharbad Founded**

Trading elf metropolis established on Vand'ardhon, becoming center of elven commerce.

**~7,500 BW — Shalazar Founded**

Halflings (the Hin) establish Shalazar as their island homeland within the elven sphere of influence.

**~7,000 BW — Height of Akovarian Civilization**

Akovarians achieve mastery of ley-line magic. Disease becomes rare, undead virtually unknown, magic flows predictably.

**~7,000 BW — Racial Territories Solidify**

Wars between humans and dwarves establish territorial boundaries. Dwarves claim mountainous regions; humans settle coastal areas. Gnomes integrate into dwarven settlements. Wemics begin to show intellect and honor among the elves, dwelling in plains regions.

**~6,900 BW — Dragons Visit Akovar**

Dragons from other continents begin visiting Akovar to learn ley-line magic from the Akovarians.

**~6,830 BW — The Dragon War**

Chromatic dragons, jealous of metallic dragons' favored status with Asti, launch coordinated campaign to destroy pyramid temples and corrupt ley-lines. The Akovarian clergy creates the Mask of the Pharaoh to hold Asti's essence.

**~6,830 BW — Asti Imprisoned; Ley-Lines Destroyed**

During the final battle at the Great Temple, priests transfer Asti's consciousness into the Mask while teleporting the population to the Ancient South. The ley-lines explode, killing most attacking dragons and destroying Akovarian cities. Survivors mutate—some becoming first dragonborn, others merging with animals. Asti is imprisoned within the Mask for the next 7,645 years.

**~6,830 BW — Varkonan Culture Emerges**

Transported Akovarians arrive in the Ancient South but suffer memory loss. High priest Thz'zar Ja'laao goes mad. Survivors eventually found Varkonan culture, guarding the Mask as sacred but poorly understood relic.

**~6,500 BW — Nepherites Founded**

Varkonan culture (Akovar refugees) establishes Nepherites in the Ancient South. Pyramid construction begins, continuing Akovarian architectural traditions.

**~6,000 BW — Mithril Discovered**

Elven mountain clans discover mithril in northern peaks of Vand'ardhon. Mountain elven culture rises to prominence.

**~5,800 BW — Uadjit Founded**

Varkonan culture expands southward; Uadjit established with genie magic traditions.

**~5,000 – 4,000 BW — First Formalized Worship Systems**

Old Gods begin rewarding followers with divine spells:
- Pyrantor worship spreads among desert/volcanic communities
- Iquinias gains veneration from coastal peoples and sailors
- Vuleala's Skysingers emerge among nomadic plainspeople
- Dirgin becomes primary deity of underground-dwelling dwarves/gnomes
- Zebulba cults operate in shadows
- Gruhathyrir's druids serve as shamans

**~4,800 BW — The Drowning Cities**

Four major elven coastal cities on Vand'ardhon destroyed by catastrophic flooding. Approximately 3,000–4,500 elves die. Triggers theological crisis about the Old Gods' nature.

# ERA OF AWAKENING

**~4,000 BW — Rise of Yrugas**

Dragons begin teaching magic to humans in what is today the Inner Kingdoms. The brutal mageocracy Yrugas is born. Leader Madron becomes legendary; the Madronian people are named after him. Note: Madron (human wizard-king) should not be confused with Mordrion (dwarven deity who arrived 1,866 BW).

**~3,700 BW — Seven Elven Nations Form**

Geographic and cultural divisions among elves crystallize into seven distinct nations: Yaara'sehan (High Elves), Tawarwaith (Wood Elves), Gaearman (Ocean Elves), Ra'Lad (Plains Elves), Faron'dir (Wandering Elves), Yavaw'dae (Valley Elves), and Aeglirech (Mountain Elves).

**~3,500 BW — Human Migration: Madronians to Sabonaar**

First major human migration wave. Madronians migrate south along western coast to Sabon Peninsula, eventually becoming the Sabonaar people.

**~3,500 BW — Human Migration: Varkonans to Lands of Honor**

Varkonans migrate east from Ancient South, eventually becoming the Ejiin people and founding the Lands of Honor civilization with new elemental religion.

**3,402 BW — Minerva Awakens (The Enlightening)**

Minerva awakens and makes first contact with elves of Vand'ardhon in "The Enlightening." Seven Nations formally recognized under Minerva's guidance, and the elves are called the Yaara'sehan ("ancient walkers"). The Church of Minerva is founded with two orders: Cundo'elda (clerics/oracles/inquisitors) and Cundo'dori (druids/rangers). The Enlightening also reaches Shalazar, where Minervan teachings syncretize with older Hin harvest and hospitality customs through druid circles called Greenwardens. This marks Year 1 of Elven Reckoning (ER) calendar.

**~3,200 BW — Guzon Arrives from Another Plane**

Entity of discipline, order, and self-perfection arrives on Mazariim from another plane. Creates Utopia—realm of eternal perfection.

**~3,200 BW — Miyasaki Shogunate Founded**

Ancient Ejiin shogunate with elemental religion established in the Lands of Honor (Yashaki Islands).

**~3,000 BW — The Sundering: Tiovin and Taovan Awaken**

A dual entity observing humanity splits into two: Tiovin (good—mercy, compassion, justice) creates Heaven and the Ethereal Plane; Taovan (evil—cruelty, domination) creates Hell and the Shadow Plane. They establish The Pact governing their eternal competition for souls. Tiovin dispatches Divine Missionaries (angels) to spread teachings of mercy; Taovan sends Tempting Devils to whisper of power.

**2,971 BW — Hinterhin Awakens**

Patron entity of halflings awakens, becoming cultural anchor for Shalazar and binding community practice to harvest and home.

**~2,900 BW — Ashura and Khan Awaken**

In the Ejiin civilization, as samurai swear oaths of honor, Ashura (honor, courage, self-sacrifice) awakens and carves Tengoku from part of Heaven (with Tiovin's blessing). When the first samurai breaks an oath, Khan stirs as dark reflection, claiming territory within Hell (with Taovan's permission). They become cosmic duality—battling eternally during lunar eclipses.

**~2,800 BW — Veppankarai Islands Settled**

Ejiin island ninja culture established, slightly younger than mainland Miyasaki Shogunate.

**~2,800 BW — Ry'le Awakens in the Far Realm**

Far Realm entity resembling a large cephalopod awakens. Unlike entities seeking worship, Ry'le seeks to replace mortal civilizations with shapeshifting creations. Watches Mazariim for ~3,600 years before making contact.

**~2,700 BW — Fall of Yrugas; Creation of the Hollow Sepulcher**

Yrugas collapses in magical catastrophe and slave revolts. Mass death creates the Hollow Sepulcher—Zebulba's greatest temple. Undead armies pour from ruins for centuries until coalition seals the breach. The Void-Touched maintain wards from inside perpetually.

**~2,650 BW — Kruta Founded**

Yrugas refugees flee south to the Lavantine Peninsula (Lands of Chaos), mixing with the native Laveri peoples and founding Kruta, continuing brutal mageocratic traditions and slavetrading practices.

**~2,500 BW — Wars of Faith Begin**

Conflicts between elemental cults and newer churches. Worshipers of Taovan and Tiovin clash across Mazariim for centuries.

**~2,500 BW — Human Migration: Madronians to Horselands**

Madronians migrate east to central plains, establishing Khonayn sacred site and eventually becoming the Chak'ti people.

**~2,250 BW — Dwarven Holds Established**

Dwarves and gnomes rise from barbarism. Clans create first extensive dwarven holds.

**~2,200 BW — Human Migration: Varkonans to Azala Region**

Varkonans migrate northeast to tropical jungles, eventually founding Azala jungle kingdom.

**~2,100 BW — Zhonhylite Founded**

Primary gnomish citadel established near dwarven territories.

**~2,000 BW — Human Migration: Madronians to Frozen Isles**

Madronians begin northern exploration, eventually settling Frozen Isles and becoming the Nond people.

**~1,982–1,912 BW — The Purge of Shadows**

On Vand'ardhon, the Hollow Brotherhood necromantic cult is discovered and destroyed in 70-year holy crusade. Over 2,000 elven casualties. The Pale Watch founded.

**1,921 BW — The Dwarven Wars Begin**

Brutal conflicts between dwarven clans over mining rights and rulership. Northern Gormdawi clans seek unification; southern Bryndawi clans resist.

**~1,900 BW — Azala Founded**

Varkonan migrants establish Azala jungle kingdom, continuing pyramid-building traditions from Akovar heritage.

# ERA OF ARRIVAL

**1,866 BW — The Wounding: Mordrion and Zuel Arrive from Oerth**

On world of Oerth, gods Moradin and Gruumsh battle. Gruumsh's spear rips out Moradin's left eye; Moradin's axe severs Gruumsh's left hand. Energy from wounds ruptures reality, hurling both into Mazariim's universe where they become mortal entities:
- **Gruumsh** becomes **Zuel the Devourer** — begins building orcish army
- **Moradin** becomes **Mordrion the Dwarf Father** — helps end Dwarven Wars

This marks Year 1 of Dwarven Reckoning (DR) calendar.

**1,849 BW — The Dwarven Wars End**

Mordrion brokers peace between Gormdawi and Bryndawi clans. Dwarven holds expand rapidly. Worship of Mordrion replaces Dirgin as dominant dwarven faith. Day of Unity holiday established.

**~1,800 BW — Javalh Founded**

First Frozen Isles settlement established by Nond migrants. Vikingesque navytown and seafaring culture develops.

**~1,780 BW — Rivoleen Awakens**

Entity of curiosity and innovation awakens, appearing to gnomes as humble scholar. Within a generation, becomes dominant gnomish deity.

**1,799 BW — The War of Souls Begins**

Formal conflict between faithful of Tiovin and Taovan across Mazariim.

**~1,750 BW — Humanity Rises; Taovan Driven Underground**

Humankind rises from barbarism. Followers of Tiovin drive cult of Taovan into shadows in most regions.

**~1,750 BW — Dammarth Founded**

Gnomish elementalist mageocracy established.

**1,666 BW — Garnak Awakens**

Entity of savage strength and predatory instinct awakens among primitive races. Worship spreads quickly among orcs, goblins, and ogres.

**~1,650 BW — Beneshire Founded**

Oldest Inner Kingdom established as traditional feudal monarchy in Madronian lands.

**~1,600 BW — Khubilai Founded**

Southern dwarven hold established, more independent than northern empire.

**~1,500 BW — Human Migration: Varkonans to Lavantine Peninsula**

Varkonans migrate north to the Lavantine Peninsula (Lands of Chaos) swamplands, mixing with native Laveri peoples and eventually fragmenting into the Ubyra tribes.

**~1,500 BW — Tortuganae Founded**

Nonhuman archipelago refuge established in Forgotten Isles.

**1,492 BW — Alagalothor, Thalaman, and Sheeva Arrive from Talernon**

Three entities from dying universe arrive on Mazariim:
- **Alagalothor** — becomes patron of arcane magic, settles in Far Realm
- **Thalaman** — fragmented across timelines, teaches time magic from Limbo
- **Sheeva** — bitter and vengeful, claims Gardens of Vitriol in Abyss; served by Demodands

**1,414 BW — Paladine Arrives from Krynn**

Paladine the Platinum Dragon manifests. The first paladin of Mazariim is anointed—a holy warrior who fights with both sword and spell, empowered by sacred oaths. Paladine shares Heaven with Tiovin. Grand Cathedral of Justice built.

**~1,400 BW — Chumbawamba Founded**

Younger gnomish settlement established.

**1,377 BW — Eilistraee Arrives from Faerun**

During Faerun's Spellplague, a fragment of Eilistraee's essence and several hundred drow refugees arrive on Mazariim. Minerva welcomes her to Elysium. First surface drow (moon drow) population established, demonstrating that dark elves can reject evil. The Moonlit Refuge Network—an underground railroad helping drow escape to the surface—is eventually established.

**1,328 BW — Sabonaa Unified**

Sabonaar culture unifies as single nation—early mercantile power and namesake for Sabonaar people.

**1,293 BW — Quarzel Awakens**

Entity of organized warfare manifests on battlefield. His first high priest is elevated to the rank of Iron Fist, first supreme commander of Quarzel's faith. Quarzel's servants, the Titans (fifty-foot-tall warriors of living stone and metal), enforce his will. Month of Quarz named in his honor.

**~1,200 BW — Brahjhin Founded**

Honorable meritocracy established in Frozen Isles among the Nond people.

**1,111 BW — Gyru Ascends to Godhood**

Mortal dwarf Gyru Ironspanner creates Apotheosis Engine to absorb magical energy. Alagalothor shatters device, but energy transforms Gyru into Lesser Entity. Gyru transported to Utopia, where he builds a clockwork laboratory served by axiomites (mathematical beings native to Utopia). Machine fragments scatter across universe.

**1,001 BW — Dukhan Empire Expansion Begins**

Theocracy based on Zebulba worship begins multi-city expansion in Sabonaar lands, eventually dominating Merchant Nations region.

**~800 BW — Graniteshield Founded**

Independent dwarven trade metropolis established.

**~750 BW — Arogror Founded**

Industrious hillsfolk nation established in Inner Kingdoms among the Madronian people.

**699 BW — Xannith Awakens**

Entity of greed awakens. Gains favor among human nobles and merchants. Establishes the Chains of Gold, a clergy of corrupt nobles and slave traders. Served by Div emissaries. Her temples (disguised as banks and auction houses) spread to every major city.

**~650 BW — Xeque Founded**

Hedonistic merchant oligarchy established in Inner Kingdoms among the Madronian people.

**555 BW — Sabonaa Fragments; Naarlam Emerges**

Sabonaa splinters into smaller nations. Plague of Madness devastates population. Naarlam emerges as one of the successor states, inheriting Sabonaa's mercantile traditions and eventually becoming home to seven noble houses with 2,000+ year lineages.

**~550 BW — Tentigo Founded**

Warmongering Sabonaar theocracy established on Sabon Peninsula.

**~500 BW — Ubyra Tribes Formalize**

Laveri swamp tribes (with Varkonan ancestry) finally unify into confederation after 1,000 years of fragmentation.

**472 BW — Holy Theocracy of Tentigo Founded; Tiovesh Named**

Militaristic Tiovinian nation formally established. Town of Vesh renamed Tiovesh as capital.

**412 BW — Cecastaa Splits from Naarlam**

Eastern Naarlam territories rebel and form Cecastaa.

**~400 BW — Sharma-Ad Formalized**

Nomadic tent-city formalized as mobile nation in the Horselands among the Chak'ti people.

**374 BW — Polyvin Awakens**

Entity of fair trade and shared prosperity awakens. Becomes enemy of Xannith. Establishes Fortune Halls—temples combining community centers, marketplaces, and gambling halls—offering practical help to common folk. Resides in the Towers of Chance.

**310 BW — Zanthar Flees Dukhan**

Zanthar flees Dukhan Empire, builds tower in Steppes.

**213 BW — Dukhan Empire Collapses**

Soros un-Kahn's dark ritual to summon Zebulba avatar fails catastrophically. Negative energies pour through, killing all living things near ziggurats. Empire destroyed; survivors flee.

**~180 BW — Zzan Founded**

Draconic fiefdom established in very cold northern region of the Lavantine Peninsula (Lands of Chaos).

# ERA OF UNITY

**4 BW (Summer) — The War to End All Wars Begins**

Zuel's million-strong horde erupts from mountains simultaneously across multiple fronts:
- Dwarven holds fall within months
- Frog-daemons emerge from oceans attacking coastal settlements
- Twelve coastal cities wiped from maps
- Civilization hangs by thread

**3 BW — The Concord of Unity Formed**

Entities become directly involved:
- Hinterhin feeds millions through blessed harvests
- Rivoleen defends Gnomish Realms
- Mordrion leads forces personally
- Half-dozen minor entities destroyed defeating ogre-magi generals
- **Minerva divides essence among Seven Artharans** rather than manifesting avatar

At Fort Hope, unprecedented alliance forms: the Concord of Unity unites Dwarves, Gnomes, Humans, Elves, and Halflings.

Elven expeditionary force of 30,000 warriors deployed to Nigal. Daemon ambush destroys 75% of elven navy; thousands of sailors die; expeditionary force stranded.

**2 BW — Final Year of the War**

Most brutal year. Zuel's avatar personally manifests and leads army toward Fort Hope.

**1 BW (Autumn) — The Battle of Fort Hope**

- Mordrion's avatar, Quarzel, and lesser entities confront Zuel
- Zuel destroys them all—**Quarzel obliterated permanently**
- **Demious Stoneshaper** wields Mordrion's blessed warhammer
- Stoneshaper shatters Zuel's avatar after hours of combat
- Zuel banished, unable to manifest avatar for 101 years
- Orcish horde breaks and flees

**3 Quarz (January 3) 1 AW — War Officially Ends**

Calendar reset. This becomes Year 1 of After War (AW) era.

**War Aftermath:**
- Demious Stoneshaper becomes king of northern dwarven realms
- Stoneshaper's Vigil holiday commemorates victory
- 12,000 elves died (40% of expeditionary force)—bloodiest elven conflict since Enlightening
- Stranded 18,000 elven survivors gradually evacuated; 500–800 choose to remain on Nigal
- Famine and disease ravage world for next decade
- Entities whose avatars were destroyed banished from manifesting for 101 years

# THE MODERN ERA

## Post-War Recovery (1 – 200 AW)

**1 AW — Founding of the Federation of Man**

In the vacuum following the War, the human city-states of the Inner Kingdoms establish a permanent confederation at Fort Hope to maintain the peace and unity forged during the Concord of Unity. The Federation is governed by the Praetorian Council, with representatives from each member state.

**~45 AW — Saerrus Founded**

Revolutionary democracy rebuilt from 2–3 Madronian nations destroyed in War.

**~88 AW — Grand Confederacy of Pragos Founded**

Industrial arcanist confederation formed from war-torn Madronian territories.

**99 AW — Cecastaa Gains Full Independence**

Final independence from Naarlam achieved.

**101 AW — The Banishment Ends**

101-year banishment of Zuel and Mordrion ends. Mordrion manifests immediately; Zuel does not. Minerva continues empowering Seven Artharans rather than manifesting directly.

**~120 AW — Medan Founded**

Survivalist meritocracy formed in Lavantine Peninsula (Lands of Chaos) from mixed Laveri War refugees.

**172 AW — First Tiovinian Crusade**

Tentigo launches first crusade.

**~180 AW — Soractian Founded**

Democratic Laveri explorers nation formed from mixed post-War populations in the Lavantine Peninsula.

**209 AW — Saerrus Revolution**

The monarchy of Saerrus falls in a popular uprising. The Federation of Man mediates peace on the borders while Saerrus transitions into an intellectual democracy, eventually solidifying its membership in the Federation.

---

## The Long Peace (200 – 700 AW)

**~200–400 AW — The Long Peace**

Longest sustained peace in recorded history. Global trade networks connect all continents. Half-elf population grows; approximately 5,000–8,000 elves live permanently on Nigal by 400 AW. Military readiness declines.

**219 AW — Second Tiovinian Crusade**

Tentigo launches second crusade.

**291 AW — Empire of Simir Founded**

King Petrov Simir of Asim begins assimilating southern Merchant Nations through economic conquest. Empire includes Amara, Instar, and Moamon. The Empire rises in part as a rival power to the Federation of Man's dominance in the north.

**~350 AW — Nond Founded**

Youngest Frozen Isles settlement established by Nond people in far northern expansion.

**376 AW — The Stone-Steel Treaty**

The Federation of Man signs a historic trade and peace treaty with the Dwarven Empire, opening formal commerce across the Dragon Spines and leading to the construction of the Great Rail—an ambitious underground railway linking human and dwarven territories.

**397 AW — Third Tiovinian Crusade**

Tentigo launches third crusade.

**~600–620 AW — The Federation Expansion**

A wave of nationalism drives the Federation to pressure the Merchant Nations (particularly Naarlam and Cecastaa) into joining. Trade blockades and border skirmishes occur, but the expansion is ultimately voted down by the Praetorian Council, leading to new trade treaties instead. The failed expansion marks a turning point toward diplomacy over coercion.

---

## Late Modern Era (686 – 810 AW)

**686 AW — Empire of Simir Falls**

King Tiberius Simir assassinated. Inter-family conflict and aggressive neighbors fragment empire. Within decade, only Asim and Moamon remain.

**689 AW — Thaumaturgic Council Takes Control of Instar**

Thaumaturgic Council era begins in Instar, transforming the city into a magocracy.

**692 AW — Free City of Amara Founded**

Independence established after Simir collapse, becoming major Sabonaar mercantile autocracy.

**711 AW — Amara Civil Wars End**

Stability restored in Free City of Amara.

**715 AW — Instar Ports Close**

Trade redirected to Amara.

**733 AW — Amara-Moamon Conflict**

Heavy losses on both sides; eventual truce.

**~738 AW — Gauntlight Ruins Created**

Gnome necromancer begins experiments in Chumbawamba, eventually creating the underground Gauntlight Ruins laboratories.

---

## Campaign Era (811 AW – Present)

**811 AW — Campaign 01: Orcs From Beyond (Orceri Incursion)**

Zanthar's Machine reawakens after ~1,122 years dormant. Orcs from alternate timeline invade. Heroes seal Orceri in their own timeline. First planar ripples since Dragon War. In the Lands of Honor, an Oni (demon-spirit, servant of Khan) mysteriously aids the defense of Kashi against Orceri attackers—an event that will have dark repercussions.

**815 AW — Campaign 02: Rebirth of Akovar**

Heroes discover lost continent of Akovar. Mask of the Pharaoh recovered from Nepherites Pyramid. **Asti freed after 7,645 years of imprisonment.** Ley-lines reawakened across Akovar. Magical shockwaves ripple across all planes.

**819 AW — Campaign 03: Wrath of the Ancients**

Noctuminalis awakens, seizes Umbral Hand. Rising tensions between Saerrus, Pragos, and Merchant Nations.

**820 AW — Ry'le Makes First Contact**

Far Realm entity awakened by planar disturbances. Contacts a Madronian warlock through dreams.

**822 AW — Ry'le Opens Rift**

Seven Masters (elite shapeshifters) created. Portal opened at Yrugas ruins in Beneshire.

**824 AW — Campaign 04: Lords of Madness**

First mortal-crafted ley-line created. Ry'le's infiltration exposed; civil war in Beneshire averted. Portal at Yrugas ruins destroyed.

**832 AW — Campaigns 05 & 06: Secrets of the Shadow Realm / Honor and Steel**

Devil Cube plot in Shadow Realm. A shaman banished twenty years earlier for attempting to harness Oni power unleashes zombie plague on Kashi in the Lands of Honor; he is killed, but thousands die.

**835 AW — Campaign 07: Cosmic Journeys**

Planar exploration, Astral Circle research. Mortal ley-line creation becomes established practice.

**836 AW — Campaign 08: Children of the Fey**

Morning Valley threatened by Feywild merge. Dangerous ritual stopped.

**837 AW — Campaign 09: Saving Phandelver**

Mining settlement crisis; lost dwarven holds explored.

**838 AW — Campaign 10: Fire & Ice**

Necromancer's ghost revived by ley-lines in Chumbawamba. Clan Azure vs Children of Pyre political crisis.

**839 AW — Campaign 11: Gods of the Fall**

Five proto-entities crash into Varkonan lands. Global race for Machine of Gyru parts. An entity seeks to drain mana and ascend. Machine parts secured and destroyed. Elves deploy 8,000 soldiers—largest mobilization since War.

**842 AW — Campaign 12: Coming of the Lines (Current)**

All previous consequences converge. Shadow dragon Noctuminalis reveals endgame: transform Mazariim into land of undead. Five simultaneous catastrophic plans thwarted. 4,500 elven dead including one Artharan. Noctuminalis destroyed or sealed. Ley-line network now spans Mazariim.
