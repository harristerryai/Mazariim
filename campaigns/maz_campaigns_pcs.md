# Player Character Profiles

## Overview

The following characters have participated in various campaigns throughout Mazariim's recent history (811-844 AW). Many have appeared in multiple campaigns, creating a living continuity across decades of adventures.

---

## Azaki Nailo
**Inquisitive Wood Elven Druid Who Changes Shape**

**Physical Description:** At almost six feet tall and of slender build, Azaki has purple eyes and green hair and dark tan skin. He wears padded armor and is usually armed with a longbow and longsword. Azaki is missing his ring finger on his right hand and is unkempt, often sleeping in his clothes or in animal form, with a leaf or twig in his hair.

**Age:** 50  
**Patron Deity:** Minerva  
**Homeland:** Tawarwaith, Vand'ardhon  
**Alignment:** Chaotic Neutral  

**Ideals:** Live and Let Live, Whimsy  
**Bonds:** Drawn to the wilderness, at one with nature  
**Personality Traits:** Reeks of wizard smoke, feels far more comfortable around animals than people  
**Flaws:** Enjoys decadent pleasures (wizard smoke addiction)  

**History:** Azaki traveled the ocean and moved with his family to Fort Hope. After some time in the city growing up, he eventually decided to leave his urban lifestyle to find himself in nature. Heading north, he eventually found himself in the northern parts of Pragos, continuing his quest for oneness with nature.

**Character Arc:** Rescue

**Campaigns:** Cosmic Journeys

## Jasper Goldfound
**Halfling Ranger Who Defends the Weak**

**Physical Description:** Standing 3 feet tall with blonde hair and blue eyes, Jasper wields two rapiers and a longbow with deadly precision.

**Age:** 30  
**Patron Deity:** Hinterhin  
**Homeland:** Shalazar  
**Alignment:** Lawful Neutral  

**Bonds:** Protection of colleagues drives all actions  
**Character Arc:** Defense

**History:** From the halfling island of Shalazar, Jasper has dedicated his life to protecting those who cannot protect themselves.

**Campaigns:** Cosmic Journeys

## Orisk Frostbead
**Hill Dwarf Priest of Guzon**

**Physical Description:** A "monk-style" character who needs no weapons. Sports a dark braided top-knot and grey beard.

**Age:** 50  
**Homeland:** Kappasaki, Tansudo  
**Patron Deity:** Guzon  
**Alignment:** Lawful Neutral  

**Philosophy:** Follows the path of logic and Zen  
**Character Arc:** Growth

**History:** From the Lands of Honor, Orisk brings the disciplined philosophy of Guzon to his adventures.

**Campaigns:** Cosmic Journeys

---

## Boris "Captain of the Sky"
**Human Druid (Weather-Beaten Doomed Soul)**

**Physical Description:** A 45-year-old man with grey curly hair, weather-beaten features, and wearing a modified captain's coat.

**Age:** 45  
**Homeland:** Merchant Nations  

**History:** Survived betrayal and Krutan slavery. His past haunts him, but he fights on to protect others from similar fates.

**Character Arc:** Restoration  
**Affliction:** PTSD from captivity

**Campaigns:** Cosmic Journeys

---

## Jewel (Gnome Mage)
**Former Prisoner of a Krutan Archmage**

**Physical Description:** Alabaster skin, emerald hair, and blue eyes. Striking and memorable appearance.

**Age:** 38  

**History:** Was once a "Jewel" in the collection of a Krutan archmage, kept under mind control. She escaped and now works in an alchemy store, paranoid about her personal space but determined to live freely.

**Personality:** Paranoid about personal space due to trauma  
**Character Arc:** Enterprise

**Campaigns:** Cosmic Journeys

## Elias Fenric
**Ocean Elven Priest of Iquinias**

**Physical Description:** Blue skin, dark blue hair, and green eyes. Clearly identifies him as one of the rare ocean elves.

**Age:** 149

**Homeland:** Saltmarsh  

**History:** Lost his parents to pirates. Driven by honor and a desire to help others avoid the pain he experienced.

**Character Arc:** Aid a Friend

**Campaigns:** Cosmic Journeys

## Agusta Odinbach II
**Madronian Evocation Mage**

**Physical Description:** 6 feet tall, blonde hair, blue eyes. Always dressed in noble attire appropriate to his station.

**Age:** 35  
**Patron Deity:** Tiovin  
**Homeland:** Arogror  
**Alignment:** Lawful Neutral  

**Personality:** Views other races as beneath him, values family bloodline above all  

**History:** From a wealthy merchant family in Arogror. Helped avert the Elemental Crisis, establishing himself as a powerful evoker and defender of the realm.

**Campaigns:** Wrath of the Ancients

## Dame Tarime Ilthrel
**Wood Elven Ranger (Hunter of Ry'lehe)**

**Physical Description:** Golden eyes and auburn hair. 5'6" tall.

**Age:** 120  
**Patron Deity:** Minerva  
**Homeland:** Iaurtorn, Vand'ardhon  

**Personality:** Highly skilled with a bow, more comfortable with animals than people  

**History:** Expert hunter who helped stop the Ry'lehe in Beneshire. Now resides with the Astral Circle, hunting aberrations.

**Campaigns:** Lords of Madness

## Character Arc Types

- **Rescue** - Driven to save others
- **Defense** - Protect the weak and innocent
- **Growth** - Personal development and enlightenment
- **Restoration** - Healing from past trauma
- **Enterprise** - Building something new
- **Aid a Friend** - Loyalty and companionship above all

These heroes represent the best of Mazariim, standing against threats both mundane and cosmic across multiple decades of world history.
