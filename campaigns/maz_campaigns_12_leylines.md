# 12 - Coming of the Lines (Final Campaign)

## Campaign Overview

The ultimate campaign where unstable ley-lines cause global chaos. Noctuminalis orchestrates five final catastrophic plans to transform Mazariim into a land of the dead. Multiple parties of high-level characters must complete five critical missions simultaneously to save the planet.

## Campaign Background

### Ley-Lines Everywhere

Due to the many reasons outlined in previous campaigns:
- The Orceri sealing sent planar ripples
- Asti's ley-lines awakened ancient knowledge
- The Astral Circle successfully created new ley-lines
- Various entities manipulated ley-line energies
- Ley-lines have arisen all over Mazariim

### Global Destruction

The proliferation of ley-lines causes widespread devastation:
- Uncontrolled magical surges
- Planar boundaries weakening
- Reality warping in ley-line intersections
- Ancient sites reactivating
- Mass casualties and destruction

### Noctuminalis's Masterplan

Behind all of this is the evil shadow dragon Noctuminalis, who has been manipulating events since the Elemental Crisis. His end goal is to cause Mazariim to transform into a land of the dead where he will reign as an undead god-king.

### The Five Catastrophes

Noctuminalis has prepared five simultaneous final strikes:

1. **Blot Out the Sun** - Create an impermeable layer blocking all sunlight
2. **Crash the Moons** - Disrupt Io'el and Randir'el's orbits
3. **Expand the Shadow Plane** - Reopen and expand the Devil Cube
4. **Banish Minerva** - Capture and imprison the aspects of Minerva
5. **Open the Far Realm** - Create a massive permanent portal

## Campaign Synopsis

### Session Zero: The Setup

Session Zero includes:
- Explanation of the multi-party structure
- World state briefing
- Stakes establishment (nothing less than planetary survival)
- Character creation or selection
- Party assignments to the five missions

### The Five Parties

This campaign requires 5-6 players each controlling characters in different parties. Alternatively, the same players can play multiple missions sequentially.

**Party Composition:**
Each party should have 3-4 high-level PCs (15th level+) specialized for their mission.

### Mission 1: Stop the Sun-Blotting Layer

**The Threat:**
Noctuminalis cultists are using corrupted ley-lines to create an impermeable magical barrier in the upper atmosphere that will block all sunlight.

**Location:** High altitude ritual sites across multiple mountains
**Challenges:**
- Extreme altitude and weather
- Flying undead and air elementals
- Multiple ritual sites that must be stopped simultaneously
- Ley-line corruption affecting magical abilities

**Success:** The layer is prevented or destroyed
**Failure:** Eternal darkness begins, undead surge

### Mission 2: Close the Devil Cube

**The Threat:**
The Devil Cube has been reopened and enhanced. It's now expanding to merge large portions of Mazariim with the Shadow Plane.

**Location:** The Lands of Chaos (original Cube location)
**Challenges:**
- Shadow Plane corruption
- Devils and kytons in massive numbers
- Reality warps and illusions
- Davos's spirit has been resurrected by Noctuminalis

**Success:** The Cube is destroyed permanently
**Failure:** Shadow Plane merger accelerates

### Mission 3: Protect the Aspects of Minerva

**The Threat:**
Noctuminalis is attempting to capture all aspects of Minerva to remove the primary force of life and healing from Mazariim.

**Location:** Multiple Minerva temples and sacred groves
**Challenges:**
- Simultaneous attacks on multiple sites
- Powerful undead and Zebulban cultists
- Betrayal from within (corrupted priests)
- Time-critical teleportation between sites

**Success:** Minerva's aspects remain free
**Failure:** Minerva is imprisoned, healing magic weakens globally

### Mission 4: Prevent the Moon Disruption

**The Threat:**
Using ley-lines, Noctuminalis plans to disrupt the orbits of both moons, causing them to crash into Mazariim or spiral away.

**Location:** Ancient astronomical sites and ley-line nodes
**Challenges:**
- Complex astronomical calculations needed
- Ritual sites on both moons (extraplanar travel required)
- Gravity and time manipulation effects
- Cultists willing to die to complete the ritual

**Success:** Moon orbits stabilized
**Failure:** Tidal chaos, potential impact event, orbital disruption

### Mission 5: Stop the Far Realm Portal

**The Threat:**
A massive permanent portal to the Far Realm is being opened. Ry'le and other aberrations will flood through in unstoppable numbers.

**Location:** The Yrugas ruins (site of the original Ry'le portal)
**Challenges:**
- Existing portal already partially open
- Aberrations and mind-controlled thralls
- Sanity-affecting environments
- Ry'le himself may manifest

**Success:** Portal is sealed forever
**Failure:** Far Realm invasion begins

### The Final Confrontation

After all five missions are complete (success or failure), the surviving heroes converge on Noctuminalis's lair for the ultimate battle against the shadow dragon.

**Noctuminalis's Power:**
- Enhanced by any successful catastrophes
- Weakened by any prevented catastrophes
- Has prepared contingencies and escape plans
- Commands legions of undead

**Victory:** Noctuminalis is destroyed, his plans ended, Mazariim saved
**Defeat:** The Age of Darkness begins, Noctuminalis ascends

## Campaign Outcomes

### Complete Victory
- All five catastrophes prevented
- Noctuminalis destroyed
- Ley-lines stabilized
- Mazariim enters a new golden age
- Heroes become legends

### Partial Victory
- Some catastrophes prevented, others succeed
- Noctuminalis defeated but escapes
- Mazariim survives but scarred
- Long recovery period needed
- Some regions permanently changed

### Failure
- Multiple catastrophes succeed
- Noctuminalis achieves apotheosis
- Mazariim becomes a nightmare realm
- Survivors flee to other planes
- The world as known is ended

## Legacy

This campaign concludes the Mazariim campaign arc spanning decades of in-game time. The outcome determines the future of the entire world and sets the stage for whatever comes next.

The heroes who participate in this campaign (successful or not) become the most legendary figures in Mazariim history, their deeds remembered for millennia.

## Player Characters

The ultimate all-star team featuring surviving heroes from all previous campaigns plus new champions. Each party of 3-4 characters represents the best hope for their assigned mission.
