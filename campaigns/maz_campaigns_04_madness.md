# 04 - Lords of Madness (824 AW)

## Campaign Overview

The entity Ry'le, awakened by planar disturbances, establishes itself in the Far Realm and begins infiltrating Beneshire through shapeshifting aberrations. A new set of heroes must stop these creatures from causing civil war and the dissolution of Beneshire.

## Campaign Background

### Ry'le Awakens

A relatively weak entity, Ry'le woke from eons of slumber and immediately found a home in one of the near-infinite nightmarish layers of the Far Realm. The planar ripples from the Orceri sealing and Asti's awakening had roused this ancient consciousness, giving it purpose and direction.

### The Warlock Berton Adluf

The entity Ry'le began by sending dream messages and spells to a Madronian warlock named Berton Adluf. The warlock, ambitious and power-hungry, became Ry'le's first willing servant in Mazariim. Through Adluf, Ry'le learned of the political landscape of Beneshire and identified an opportunity for infiltration.

### The Seven Masters

Ry'le created seven powerful servants known as the "Masters"—shapeshifting aberrations capable of perfectly mimicking humanoid forms. These creatures were sent through rifts in the Plane of Dreams into Beneshire, specifically targeting the rival noble houses of Halycon and Renoalt.

### The Infiltration

The Masters and their thralls began systematically replacing key individuals in both noble houses with perfect copies. The replaced victims were not killed but transformed into mindless thralls, their personalities subsumed into the Ry'lehe hivemind. The goal was to incite a civil war between the two houses that would tear Beneshire apart, allowing Ry'le to expand its influence throughout the region.

### The Astral Circle's Ley-Line

During the heroes' efforts to help the Astral Circle restart a small ley-line, the presence of the Ry'lehe was revealed. The magical energies exposed the psychic connections between the thralls and their masters, allowing the heroes to finally understand the true nature of the threat.

## Campaign Synopsis

### Campaign Structure

The campaign begins with mundane adventures to introduce players to Beneshire, slowly revealing the horrific truth of the Far Realm infiltration.

### Phase 1: Introduction to Beneshire

The campaign begins with a few adventures to introduce the players to Beneshire and its major players.

**Early Adventures:**
- Arrive in Littlebridge and meet local nobles
- Investigate minor crimes and disturbances
- Interact with the Astral Circle of mages
- Begin to notice subtle inconsistencies in people's behavior
- Discover the first hints of shapeshifting creatures

### Phase 2: The Village Liberation

**Discovering the Thralls:**
The heroes eventually discover their own home village has been infiltrated. Friends, family, and neighbors have been replaced by sullen, emotionless versions of themselves.

**Key Objectives:**
- Identify who has been replaced
- Find the Master controlling the village thralls
- Liberate villagers from the hivemind
- Discover the extent of the infiltration

### Phase 3: The Noble Houses

**Preventing Civil War:**
With the village liberated, the heroes must alert the noble houses of Halycon and Renoalt that they have been infiltrated. This is complicated by the fact that some nobles are themselves thralls who will work to discredit the heroes.

**Challenges:**
- Prove the existence of the infiltration without seeming insane
- Identify which nobles have been replaced
- Navigate court politics while hunting shapeshifters
- Prevent provocations designed to start the civil war
- Gather allies among the uncompromised nobility

### Phase 4: The Yrugas Ruins

**The Source of the Incursion:**
Investigation reveals that the shapeshifters are entering Mazariim through a rift in the ancient Yrugas ruins. The heroes must venture into these dangerous ruins to close the portal.

**The Ruins:**
- Navigate trap-filled ancient architecture
- Face aberrations guarding the approach to the rift
- Discover the history of previous attempts to contact the Far Realm
- Locate the ritual site maintaining the portal

### Phase 5: Destroying the Masters

**The Final Hunt:**
To truly end the threat, the heroes must hunt down and destroy at least four of the seven Masters. These powerful aberrations have hidden themselves throughout Beneshire, each controlling networks of thralls.

**Master Locations:**
- Hidden within noble estates
- Masquerading as merchants or guild leaders
- Operating from secret cult temples
- Lurking in the sewers and undercity

**Victory Condition:**
If the heroes can manage to kill at least four of the Masters, the others will flee from Beneshire, severing their connection to their thralls and ending the immediate threat.

## Campaign Outcomes

### Success

**If Heroes Defeat Four+ Masters:**
- The remaining Masters flee Beneshire
- Thralls are freed from the hivemind (though traumatized)
- The portal in the Yrugas ruins is closed
- Civil war is averted
- The noble houses begin to heal their relationship
- The Astral Circle gains valuable knowledge about Far Realm incursions

### Partial Success

**If Fewer Masters Are Defeated:**
- Some Masters remain in hiding
- Pockets of thralls continue operating
- Political tensions remain high
- The threat is reduced but not eliminated
- Ongoing vigilance is required

### The Ley-Line Discovery

During the campaign, the heroes help the Astral Circle successfully restart a small ley-line. This achievement provides:
- Enhanced magical research capabilities
- A focal point for defending against future incursions
- Early warning system for planar disturbances
- Foundation for future ley-line projects

## Legacy

The Lords of Madness campaign establishes:
- The Far Realm as an ongoing threat to Mazariim
- The Ry'lehe as a persistent antagonist
- The fragility of political structures to infiltration
- The importance of the Astral Circle in defending against planar threats

The heroes who stopped the Ry'lehe become recognized experts on aberrations and Far Realm phenomena, knowledge that will prove valuable in future crises.

## Player Characters

A new generation of heroes:
- **Dame Tarime Ilthrel** (Wood Elven Ranger) - Expert hunter of Ry'lehe aberrations
- **Lady Jala of Littlebridge** (Madronian Mage) - Instrumental in stopping the Ry'lehe
- **Sir Khalic O'Grady** (Mountain Dwarf Fighter) - Helped prevent the takeover of Beneshire
- And other brave souls who faced the horror from beyond
