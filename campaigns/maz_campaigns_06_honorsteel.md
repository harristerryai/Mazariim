# 06 - Honor and Steel (832 AW)

## Campaign Overview

In the far-off Ejiin lands (Lands of Honor), the banished shaman Soni Sabu unleashes an army of plague zombies and summoned oni in a revenge plot spanning two decades. This time-sensitive campaign focuses on stopping the spread before the Phoenix Clan falls.

## Campaign Background

### The Orceri Incursion (811 AW) - Twenty Years Earlier

During the Orceri Incursion, the Ejiin city of Kashi came under attack. In its darkest hour, Khan's avatar—a powerful oni—appeared and helped defend the city from the invaders. The oni's intervention turned the tide, saving thousands of lives.

### Soni Sabu's Heresy

Soni Sabu, a shaman who witnessed Khan's avatar in action, became convinced that the oni could be manipulated and controlled for the benefit of the Ejiin people. He suggested to his clan elders that they should study ways to summon and bind oni to serve as protectors and warriors.

This suggestion was considered blasphemous. Khan is the entity of dishonor and evil in Ejiin religion, and deliberately calling upon his servants was an unforgivable act. Soni Sabu was banished from his clan in disgrace.

### Twenty Years of Preparation

Filled with rage and resentment, Soni Sabu spent the next twenty years in exile:
- Studying forbidden rituals to summon oni
- Developing a magical disease called the "Taint"
- Perfecting techniques to raise plague zombies
- Planning revenge against the Phoenix Clan that banished him
- Building secret shrines and summoning circles

### The Taint - The Zombie Plague

Soni Sabu created a supernatural disease that:
- Spreads through contact with infected victims
- Weakens the living and kills them within days
- Causes the dead to rise as plague zombies
- Cannot be easily cured by conventional magic
- Spreads exponentially if not contained

### The Spider Oni

Using his mastered rituals, Soni Sabu summoned dozens of spider-like oni into the world. These creatures serve as:
- Shock troops and assassins
- Spreaders of the Taint
- Commanders for zombie hordes
- Guardians of summoning sites

## Campaign Synopsis

### The Timeline - Time Is Critical

This campaign operates on a strict timeline. Soni Sabu has a schedule of actions to achieve his goals, and the players must race to stop him before each deadline.

**Soni Sabu's Timeline:**

1. **Day 1-3: Imigan** - Release the Taint, await dying to raise as plague zombies
2. **Day 4-6: Nanishu** - Summon Commander Oni to lead the growing horde
3. **Day 7-9: Chimi** - Summon Water Oni to spread disease via waterways
4. **Day 10-12: Kashiro** - Summon Fire Oni to burn defenses
5. **Day 13-15: Kashi** - Summon Warrior Oni for final assault on Phoenix Clan capital

### Campaign Structure

**Phase 1: The Outbreak (Imigan)**
- Heroes arrive at Imigan to investigate reports of disease
- Discover the first plague zombies
- Learn of Soni Sabu's involvement
- Attempt to contain the initial outbreak
- Race toward Nanishu to prevent the next summoning

**Phase 2: The Commander (Nanishu)**
- Arrive at Nanishu to find the ritual in progress or completed
- Face the Commander Oni if the ritual succeeded
- Gather intelligence on Soni Sabu's plan
- Try to get ahead of the schedule

**Phase 3: The Waterways (Chimi)**
- Prevent the Water Oni summoning if possible
- If failed, fight to destroy the Water Oni before it corrupts major water sources
- Evacuate threatened villages
- Coordinate with local authorities

**Phase 4: The Fires (Kashiro)**
- Stop the Fire Oni ritual
- Defend against combined zombie and oni attacks
- Learn Soni Sabu's final target: Kashi
- Prepare for the final confrontation

**Phase 5: The Final Battle (Kashi)**
- Race to Kashi before the Warrior Oni summoning
- Defend the Phoenix Clan capital
- Find and confront Soni Sabu himself
- Stop the final ritual and destroy the shaman

### Key Locations

**Imigan** - Small fishing village, ground zero for the Taint
**Nanishu** - Trading town, site of the Commander Oni summoning
**Chimi** - River settlement, targeted for water contamination
**Kashiro** - Fortified town, important defensive position
**Kashi** - Capital of the Phoenix Clan, final target

### Oni Types

**Spider Oni** - Initial shock troops, spread the Taint
**Commander Oni** - Tactical leader for the zombie horde
**Water Oni** - Contaminates water supplies, spreads disease
**Fire Oni** - Destroys fortifications and causes chaos
**Warrior Oni** - Powerful combatant meant to kill clan leadership

## Campaign Outcomes

### Complete Victory
- Stop all or most oni summonings
- Contain the plague outbreak quickly
- Defeat Soni Sabu before the final ritual
- Minimize civilian casualties
- Save the Phoenix Clan from destruction

### Partial Success
- Some oni are summoned and cause damage
- Plague spreads to multiple settlements
- Soni Sabu escapes or is stopped at great cost
- Phoenix Clan survives but is weakened

### Failure
- Multiple oni rampage across the land
- Plague becomes widespread
- Phoenix Clan leadership is killed
- Regional collapse and humanitarian crisis

## Legacy

This campaign establishes:
- The danger of forbidden summoning magic
- The complexity of Ejiin religion and honor
- Long-term consequences of banishment and exile
- The destructive power of oni when unleashed
- The importance of acting quickly in plague scenarios

The defeat of Soni Sabu becomes a cautionary tale in Ejiin culture about the dangers of consorting with Khan's servants, even with good intentions.

## Player Characters

New heroes, possibly including Ejiin natives and foreigners who happened to be in the region:
- Skilled warriors familiar with oni legends
- Healers attempting to cure the Taint
- Investigators tracking Soni Sabu's movements
- Diplomats trying to organize regional defense
