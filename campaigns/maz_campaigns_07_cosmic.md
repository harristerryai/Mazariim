# 07 - Cosmic Journeys (835 AW - Present)

## Campaign Overview

High-level planar exploration campaign where heroes from two previous campaigns join forces with the Astral Circle to research and protect a fledgling ley-line. Four antagonist groups compete for control or study of the ley-line, leading to adventures across multiple planes of existence.

## Campaign Background

### The Beneshire Ley-Line (824 AW)

It was in the year 824 that the Astral Circle successfully awakened a minor ley-line in the mountains of Beneshire. This achievement, accomplished with the help of heroes during the Lords of Madness campaign, represented the first successful mortal-created ley-line since ancient Akovar.

### The Ley-Line's Significance

The ley-line is significant for multiple reasons:
- Proof that mortals can create ley-lines without divine entities
- A focal point for magical research and power
- A potential gateway for planar travel and communication
- A beacon visible to magical creatures across planes
- A strategic resource worth controlling or destroying

### Four Factions with Four Agendas

**1. The Umbral Hand (Noctuminalis)**
The shadow dragon Noctuminalis, operating through the Zebulban cult, sees the ley-line as a threat to his long-term plans. He seeks to either control it or destroy it before it can be used against him.

**2. The Dark Whispers**
A mysterious cult that worships entities from the Abyss. They want to corrupt the ley-line to create a permanent portal to the Abyss, allowing demon lords to enter Mazariim.

**3. The Nergalu Dragons**
A council of ancient dragons who believe ley-lines should only be controlled by dragons, as in ancient Akovar. They consider mortal use of ley-lines to be theft of draconic birthright.

**4. The Watchers (Acular's Faction)**
Time-traveling enigmatic observers led by the entity Acular. They study the ley-line to understand how it might affect future timelines. They neither help nor hinder but test those who interact with the ley-line.

## Campaign Synopsis

### Campaign Structure

This is a high-level campaign featuring heroes from multiple previous campaigns working together. The adventures involve defending the ley-line from various threats while exploring planar mysteries.

### Adventure 1: First Journey to the Astral Plane

**The Mission:**
The heroes attempt their first trip to the Astral Plane using the ley-line as an anchor point. This serves as both research and a test of the ley-line's capabilities.

**Challenges:**
- Navigate the strange physics of the Astral Plane
- Encounter astral entities and travelers
- Discover Noctuminalis's agents spying on the ley-line
- Return safely with newfound knowledge

**Outcome:**
Success means the heroes thwart Noctuminalis's spying operation and prove the ley-line can facilitate planar travel.

### Adventure 2: The Sphere of Annihilation

**The Threat:**
The Dark Whispers have acquired or created a Sphere of Annihilation and plan to use it to widen a portal to the Abyss.

**The Mission:**
Travel to the Abyss itself to stop the cult before they can deploy the sphere.

**Challenges:**
- Survive the hostile environment of the Abyss
- Navigate demon-controlled territory
- Infiltrate the Dark Whispers stronghold
- Destroy or redirect the Sphere of Annihilation
- Escape before demon lords notice the intrusion

### Adventure 3: Rescue from the City of Fortune

**The Situation:**
Several Astral Circle wizards have been captured and imprisoned in the City of Fortune, an extra-dimensional gambling city where debts are paid in years of servitude.

**The Mission:**
Infiltrate the City of Fortune and free the imprisoned wizards without starting a war with the city's powerful operators.

**Challenges:**
- Navigate the city's complex social and legal structures
- Win or cheat at high-stakes games to gain favor
- Locate the imprisoned wizards
- Negotiate or fight for their release
- Avoid becoming trapped in debt yourself

### Adventure 4: Tests of the Watchers

**The Encounter:**
The Watchers, led by Acular, have been observing the heroes' actions. They initiate a series of tests to determine if the heroes are worthy of protecting the ley-line.

**The Tests:**
- Combat trials against time-displaced opponents
- Moral dilemmas with no clear right answer
- Puzzles involving causality and temporal mechanics
- Demonstrations of judgment and wisdom

**Outcome:**
Passing the tests earns the Watchers' neutrality (they won't interfere). Failing might result in the Watchers actively working against the heroes or the ley-line.

### Adventure 5: The Dragon Council

**The Confrontation:**
The Nergalu dragon council issues an ultimatum: surrender the ley-line to draconic control or face war.

**The Final Battle:**
The heroes must face three ancient dragons in combat, negotiation, or some combination thereof.

**Resolution Options:**
- **Combat:** Defeat all three dragons (extremely difficult)
- **Negotiation:** Convince the dragons that mortal stewardship is acceptable
- **Compromise:** Establish shared oversight with draconic advisors
- **Clever Solutions:** Find creative ways to satisfy draconic honor without surrendering control

## Campaign Outcomes

### Complete Success
- Ley-line remains under Astral Circle control
- All four factions are either defeated, convinced, or neutralized
- Heroes gain reputation as planar champions
- Foundation laid for more mortal-created ley-lines

### Partial Success
- Ley-line survives but with compromises (shared control, restrictions)
- Some factions remain active threats
- Knowledge gained but at significant cost

### Failure
- Ley-line is destroyed, corrupted, or seized
- Factions gain power and influence
- Planar threats increase
- Future campaigns must deal with consequences

## Legacy

This campaign establishes:
- Mortals can successfully create and defend ley-lines
- The planar community takes Mazariim more seriously
- Multiple factions continue to have interest in ley-line technology
- The heroes as champions capable of operating on a planar scale

The success or failure of this campaign directly impacts the final campaign (Coming of the Lines) where ley-lines become a global phenomenon.
