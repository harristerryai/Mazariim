# 10 - Fire & Ice

## Campaign Overview

A megadungeon crawl in the Abomination Vaults (Gauntlight Ruins) combined with political intrigue in the gnomish city of Chumbawamba. The ghost of mad necromancer Salvatore Togglesprocket has been revitalized by ley-lines, while political tensions between Clan Azure and the Children of Pyre threaten civil war.

## Campaign Background

### Salvatore Togglesprocket

The story begins nearly a hundred years ago. Salvatore was a brilliant but disturbed Rothgrom (gnome) who was educated at the wizard schools in Chumbawamba. His genius was matched only by his instability and obsession with necromancy.

### The Ruins

Salvatore created a complex of underground laboratories and necromantic workshops in what are now called the Gauntlight Ruins. His experiments grew increasingly dark and dangerous until they ultimately destroyed him—or so everyone thought.

### Ley-Line Resurrection

The ley-line's endpoints extended all the way to the Gnomish Lands, intersecting the ruins of Salvatore Togglesprocket's laboratories. The magical energies brought his ghost back slowly, along with reanimating his failed experiments and creations.

### Political Crisis in Chumbawamba

Simultaneously, political tensions have escalated in the capital city:

**Clan Azure:** Traditional gnomish values, arcane research, clockwork innovation
**Children of Pyre:** Radical faction seeking to harness elemental fire magic for industrial revolution

The two factions are on the brink of civil war, with agents from each side sabotaging the other.

## Campaign Synopsis

### Dual Structure

This campaign alternates between:
1. **Megadungeon Exploration** - Delving into the Abomination Vaults
2. **City Intrigue** - Preventing civil war in Chumbawamba

### The Abomination Vaults

The heroes are recruited by the Church of Alagolothor to explore the ruins and contain whatever has been reawakened.

**The Vault Levels:**
- Upper levels: Recently reanimated undead and constructs
- Middle levels: Salvatore's experimental chambers with horrific results
- Lower levels: Reality-warping magical zones and Salvatore's ghost
- Depths: Connections to extra-dimensional spaces and elder horrors

**Progressive Threats:**
- Each vault level introduces new types of undead and aberrations
- Salvatore's ghost appears periodically, testing the heroes
- Ancient wards and traps still function
- Reality itself becomes unstable in deeper levels

### City Intrigue

Whenever the heroes return to Chumbawamba to rest and resupply, they encounter escalating political crises:

**Early Signs:**
- Rhetorical clashes between factions
- Economic sabotage
- Propaganda campaigns
- Public demonstrations

**Escalation:**
- Acts of violence
- Attempted assassinations
- Sabotage of critical infrastructure
- Formation of militant wings

**Major Antagonists:**
**Xogim Flintsweeper** - Clan Azure radical who wants to purge all "fire heretics"
**Bombus Clockmort** - Children of Pyre fanatic planning a coup

**The Heroes' Role:**
- Investigate sabotage incidents
- Protect key political figures
- Gather evidence of manipulation
- Mediate between factions
- Uncover outside agitators (possibly connected to Salvatore's return)

### The Connection

The campaign's two threads eventually connect:
- Salvatore's return is destabilizing the ley-line
- The ley-line instability is being manipulated by one or both political factions
- Artifacts from the vaults are being used to fuel the political conflict
- Or: The political conflict is distracting from the real threat in the vaults

## Campaign Outcomes

### Complete Victory
- Salvatore's ghost is laid to rest
- The Abomination Vaults are cleansed or sealed
- Civil war is averted through exposure of manipulators
- Clan Azure and Children of Pyre reach compromise
- Chumbawamba remains stable

### Partial Success
- Vaults are partially cleared but threats remain
- Civil unrest continues but major violence avoided
- One faction gains dominance over the other
- Political reforms are promised but not guaranteed

### Failure
- Salvatore's ghost escapes or grows stronger
- Vault horrors spread beyond the ruins
- Civil war erupts in Chumbawamba
- Gnomish civilization fractures
- Regional instability

## Legacy

This campaign establishes:
- Necromantic threats can be revived by ley-lines
- Gnomish political complexity and internal conflicts
- The Abomination Vaults as a legendary dungeon
- Long-term consequences of dabbling in necromancy
- How ley-lines can have unintended consequences

## Player Characters

Heroes hired by the Church of Alagolothor or invested in gnomish politics, capable of handling both dungeon crawling and social intrigue.
