# 11 - Gods of the Fall

## Campaign Overview

Five proto-entities from interstellar space crash into the Varkonan lands. Khemset, one of these beings, seeks pieces of the ancient "Machine of Gyru" to drain mana from others and ascend to godhood. Heroes race to collect the machine parts before Khemset does.

## Campaign Background

### The Cosmic Birth

A cloud of mana had spent tens of thousands of years coalescing into consciousness in the depths of interstellar space. As it approached self-awareness, something separated the cloud into five distinct sections, creating five distinct personas that continued to develop and grow independently.

### The Five Proto-Entities

**Zonth** - Entity of order and structure
**Khemset** - Entity of hunger and acquisition  
**Menot** - Entity of change and adaptation
**Nakar** - Entity of destruction and entropy
**Anumata** - Entity of harmony and balance

### The Crash

They formed physical manifestations and immediately came crashing down to Mazariim like meteors. Their impact sites were scattered across the Varkonan desert lands, each creating a crater and releasing massive amounts of magical energy.

### The Machine of Gyru

Khemset discovered knowledge of an ancient machine created by the primordial being Gyru. This device, if reassembled, can drain mana from other entities and transfer it to the machine's operator. Khemset believes this machine is the key to achieving true godhood.

### The Five Parts

The Machine of Gyru was disassembled and its five parts scattered across Mazariim:
1. **Part in Nond** (Frozen Isles)
2. **Part in Azala** (Mespoian Peninsula)
3. **Part in Instar** (Merchant Nations)
4. **Part in Graniteshield** (Dwarven Nations)
5. **Part in ???** (Final location varies)

## Campaign Synopsis

### Campaign Structure

The campaign begins in the Varkonan village of Osymunhotep and becomes a race across the globe to find the machine parts before Khemset does.

### Adventure 1: Osymunhotep Awakening

**The Opening:**
- The heroes are in Osymunhotep when the five entities crash
- Investigate the nearest impact site
- First encounter with one of the proto-entities
- Learn of Khemset's quest for the Machine
- Discover that other entities may oppose Khemset

**Key Information Gained:**
- The nature of the proto-entities
- The existence of the Machine of Gyru
- The locations of the five parts
- The stakes: if Khemset succeeds, he becomes unstoppable

### Adventure 2: The Yak-Men

**Desert Threats:**
In the Varkonan deserts, the heroes learn of one of the unique enemies of the region: the Yak-Men, brutal raiders and slavers who control portions of the desert.

**Complications:**
- Yak-Men are also searching for machine parts
- They worship destruction and may ally with Nakar
- They control key routes to some part locations
- Heroes must either fight through or negotiate passage

### Adventures 3-7: The Global Race

The campaign becomes a race to gather machine parts across Mazariim:

**Frozen Isles (Nond):**
- Arctic survival challenges
- Nond barbarian tribes
- Ancient vaults buried in ice
- Zonth's influence in the region

**Mespoian Peninsula (Azala):**
- Desert navigation
- Azala city-states politics
- Ancient Zalato ruins
- Menot's experiments with local populace

**Merchant Nations (Instar):**
- Urban investigation
- Thaumaturgic Council interference
- Vault beneath the city
- Anumata attempting to broker peace

**Dwarven Nations (Graniteshield):**
- Underground complexes
- Dwarven clan politics
- Ancient forges and workshops
- Possible alliance with Zonth

**Final Location:**
- The last part's location is revealed only after collecting the others
- Ultimate confrontation with Khemset
- Choice of what to do with the assembled machine

### The Other Entities

**Zonth** - May become an ally if shown the heroes bring order
**Menot** - Unpredictable, might help or hinder based on curiosity
**Nakar** - Actively works to destroy the machine
**Anumata** - Seeks to prevent any one entity from dominating

## Campaign Outcomes

### Multiple Endings

**Heroes Collect All Parts:**
- Destroy the machine (recommended by Anumata)
- Use it to ascend a PC to godhood (temptation)
- Bargain with one of the proto-entities
- Hide the parts in separate secure locations

**Khemset Gets Two+ Parts:**
- He begins hunting dragons for their mana
- Becomes exponentially more powerful
- May achieve apotheosis
- Heroes must find a way to stop a proto-god

**Machine Is Destroyed:**
- Proto-entities remain at their current power level
- They gradually integrate into Mazariim's pantheon
- Balance is maintained
- Potential future allies or enemies

**PC Ascends:**
- One hero becomes a demigod
- Other entities react (alliance, rivalry, or war)
- Player continues as NPC deity
- Massive cosmic consequences

## Legacy

This campaign establishes:
- New cosmic entities in Mazariim
- The potential for mortal ascension to godhood
- Ancient machines of incredible power
- Global-scale adventures across all continents
- The beginning of a new pantheon forming

The proto-entities become permanent fixtures in Mazariim cosmology, their choices during this campaign determining their future relationships with the world.

## Player Characters

High-level heroes capable of global travel and standing against god-like entities.
