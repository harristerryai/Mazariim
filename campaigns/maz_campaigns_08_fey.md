# 08 - Children of the Fey

## Campaign Overview

A green dragon named Xunog and his ally Azolova attempt to transform Morning Valley into part of the Feywild by performing rituals at ancient druid menhirs. Heroes must disrupt three summoning rituals during full moons to prevent the permanent transformation.

## Campaign Background

### Xunog the Green Dragon

Xunog is a green dragon who wanted to continue his devotion to Gruhathyrir (an ancient entity). Unable to worship directly, he sought alternative ways to demonstrate his devotion through grand magical works.

### Azolova the Fey

Azolova is a powerful fey creature from the Feywild who sees an opportunity to expand her realm into the Prime Material Plane. She has formed an alliance with Xunog, promising him power and prestige in exchange for his help.

### The Plan

By summoning fey from the Feywild using rituals at three sets of ancient druid menhirs, the two villains hope to create an area that overlaps into the extra-dimensional plane itself. If successful, Morning Valley will become part of the Feywild permanently, with reality itself transforming.

### The Menhirs

Three ancient stone circles exist in Morning Valley:
- Each was created by ancient druids for celestial observation
- Each is aligned with powerful ley-line intersections
- Each must be activated during a full moon for maximum effect
- Together, they can tear reality and merge planes

### Allied Forces

**Kobolds:** Fanatically loyal to Xunog, guarding the eastern menhir
**Lizardmen:** Allied with the dragon, protecting the western menhir
**Hermit Druid:** A corrupted druid controls the northern menhir
**Fey Creatures:** Increasing numbers as rituals succeed

## Campaign Synopsis

### Campaign Structure

The campaign revolves around three critical full moons. The heroes must stop the summoning rituals at each menhir circle before all three are completed.

### The First Full Moon

**The Discovery:**
- Strange fey appearances in Morning Valley
- Locals report missing livestock and odd occurrences
- Heroes discover the plot and the first menhir location
- First attempt to disrupt the ritual

**Challenges:**
- Find the menhir before the full moon
- Fight through kobold defenders
- Disrupt the ritual or defeat those conducting it
- Learn about the remaining two menhirs

**If Successful:** The first summoning fails
**If Failed:** Fey presence increases dramatically; reality begins to warp in one area

### The Second Full Moon

**Escalation:**
- Heroes now know the timeline and stakes
- Must locate and assault the second menhir
- Lizardmen defenders are more organized than kobolds
- Xunog may make a personal appearance to taunt the heroes

**Challenges:**
- Navigate swamp terrain controlled by lizardmen
- Overcome better-organized defenses
- Face more powerful fey that crossed over from the first ritual
- Prevent the second summoning

**If Successful:** Only one summoning completed; transformation still preventable
**If Failed:** Two summonings complete; the third will seal Morning Valley's fate

### The Third Full Moon

**The Final Battle:**
- All forces converge on the third menhir
- Xunog and Azolova personally oversee the ritual
- Fey army defends the site
- Reality is already unstable from previous summonings

**The Confrontation:**
- Fight through fey army
- Overcome the corrupted hermit druid
- Face Xunog and Azolova simultaneously
- Disrupt the final ritual

**Resolution:**
If the heroes stop the third ritual:
- Morning Valley remains in the Prime Material Plane
- Fey influence recedes over time
- Xunog and Azolova are defeated or retreat

If the third ritual succeeds:
- Morning Valley transforms into part of the Feywild
- Residents must adapt to fey reality
- Xunog and Azolova rule the transformed region
- Future campaigns must deal with a Feywild enclave in Mazariim

### Faction Leaders

**Krakatoa:** Kobold champion leading the eastern defenses
**Largetha:** Lizardman chieftain commanding the west
**The Hermit Druid:** Corrupted nature priest at the north
**Xunog:** Green dragon mastermind
**Azolova:** Fey princess orchestrating the summoning

### Optional Side Objectives

**Defeat Faction Leaders Early:**
Defeating Krakatoa, Largetha, or the Hermit Druid before their respective full moons reduces the defenders at each menhir.

**Gather Allies:**
- Convince uncorrupted druids to help
- Rally Morning Valley defenders
- Seek aid from the Astral Circle
- Recruit fey opposed to Azolova's plans

## Campaign Outcomes

### Complete Victory
- All three rituals disrupted
- Xunog and Azolova defeated or driven off
- Morning Valley saved
- Ancient menhirs protected for future use

### Partial Success
- One or two rituals completed
- Permanent fey-touched areas in the valley
- Xunog/Azolova escape but plans are thwarted
- Ongoing fey incursions

### Failure
- All three rituals completed
- Morning Valley becomes part of the Feywild
- Mass displacement or transformation of residents
- Xunog and Azolova establish a Feywild kingdom

## Legacy

This campaign establishes:
- The vulnerability of planar boundaries
- Ancient druid sites as powerful magical focal points
- Dragons can ally with extra-planar forces
- The Feywild as both beautiful and dangerous
- Consequences of failed summonings/rituals

## Player Characters

New heroes familiar with nature magic and fey lore, possibly including druids, rangers, and those with connections to ancient traditions.
