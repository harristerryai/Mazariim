# 05 - Secrets of the Shadows (832 AW)

## Campaign Overview

An ancient artifact unearthed by earthquakes from the Elemental Crisis merges a portion of the Lands of Chaos with the Shadow Plane. This Mazariim adaptation of "Curse of Strahd" replaces vampires with devils and kytons, creating a nightmarish realm where the heroes must destroy the Devil Cube and its master Davos.

## Campaign Background

### The Brothers: Salacan and Davos

The story begins with two brothers, Salacan and Davos, both ambitious mages in the Lands of Chaos. Salacan was the elder, talented and respected, while Davos lived in his brother's shadow, consumed by jealousy and resentment.

### The Taovanian Corruption

Davos was befriended by a Taovanian cultist who played upon Davos's weaknesses and desires for magical power. The cultist promised Davos that he could surpass his brother through the dark gifts of Taovan, the entity of tyranny and oppression.

### The Ritual Sacrifice

Driven by ambition and corrupted by Taovanian promises, Davos kidnapped his brother Salacan and killed him in a ritual sacrifice to Taovan. The ritual was meant to grant Davos immense power, but Taovan had other plans.

### The Devil Cube

Instead of power, Davos found himself imprisoned within the Devil Cube, a powerful artifact created by Taovan himself. The Devil Cube is an extradimensional space that does not allow anyone imprisoned within it to escape. However, it also serves as a lure—the Cube creates an attractive exterior that draws others inside, where they are transformed by the Shadow Plane's corruption.

### The Earthquake and the Emergence

For centuries, the Devil Cube lay buried and forgotten. Then came the Elemental Crisis (819 AW), with its earthquakes and upheavals. One of these quakes unearthed the Cube, and its malevolent influence began to spread.

### The Shadow Plane Merger

The Devil Cube's power merged a small portion of the Lands of Chaos with the Shadow Plane, creating a nightmarish overlap where:
- Daylight is perpetually dim
- Undead are replaced by devils and kytons (chain devils)
- Reality itself is twisted and unstable
- Those who enter become sullen, hopeless versions of themselves

### Davos's Current State

Trapped within the Cube, Davos has become something less than human but more than a mere prisoner. He rules the nightmare realm from his fortress, eternally seeking escape while punishing all who enter for his own fate. Like Strahd, he has become obsessed with a woman—in this case, Tatyana, a Seeker of Asti who wandered into the cursed lands.

## Campaign Synopsis

### Campaign Structure

This campaign closely follows the structure of "Curse of Strahd" but with Mazariim-specific modifications.

### The Spike of Asti (False Identity)

The campaign begins with the Devil Cube masquerading as the "Spike of Asti," a supposed artifact that promises healing and restoration. This false identity attracts adventurers, pilgrims, and seekers of Asti's blessing—all of whom become trapped once they enter.

### Entering the Cursed Lands

**Initial Hook:**
- Heroes hear rumors of a miraculous healing artifact
- They travel to investigate, hoping to use it for good
- Upon entering the region, reality shifts
- The heroes realize they are trapped in a shadowy nightmare realm

### The Shadow-Touched Lands

The affected region exhibits the following characteristics:
- Constant dim twilight, even at midday
- Mists that confuse direction and bring visitors deeper inward
- Villages full of sullen, hopeless people
- Devils and kytons instead of undead
- Three key artifacts needed to defeat Davos

### The Three Artifacts (Player Goals)

**The Holy Symbol of Tiovin**
A powerful relic that can protect against infernal corruption and weaken Davos's hold on the realm.

**The Tome of Davos**
Davos's own spellbook and journal, containing the history of his fall and clues to his weaknesses.

**The Sunsword**
A weapon of pure radiance that can cut through shadow magic and harm beings of darkness.

### Tatyana - The Seeker of Asti

Tatyana is a Seeker of Asti (similar to Ireena Kolyana in Strahd) who Davos has become obsessed with. She represents everything Davos lost—purity, hope, and connection to the light.

**Her Role:**
- Davos seeks to possess her as his bride
- The heroes must protect her while finding the artifacts
- Her fate is tied to the resolution of the campaign
- She may hold the final key to destroying the Cube

### Davos as the Villain

Davos should be a constant presence throughout the campaign, much as Strahd is in the original adventure:
- He appears at dramatic moments
- He toying with the heroes, testing them
- He extends false offers of alliance
- He genuinely believes he is the victim of his brother's superiority
- He sees the heroes as potential allies or threats to be eliminated

### The Final Confrontation

With all three artifacts assembled, the heroes must:
1. Assault Davos's fortress (analogous to Castle Ravenloft)
2. Navigate trapped and corrupted halls filled with devils
3. Confront Davos in his throne room
4. Destroy Davos, which will destroy the Devil Cube
5. Free Tatyana and all trapped souls

## Campaign Outcomes

### Victory

**If Davos Is Destroyed:**
- The Devil Cube shatters and ceases to exist
- The Shadow Plane merger ends
- The cursed lands return to normal (though scarred)
- Trapped souls are freed
- Tatyana is saved and can return to Asti's service
- A stunning defeat for the Taovinians

### Partial Victory

**If Davos Is Weakened But Escapes:**
- The Cube is damaged but not destroyed
- Most souls are freed but some remain trapped
- The Shadow Plane influence is reduced but lingers
- Davos may return in a future campaign

### Failure

**If the Heroes Fall:**
- They join the ranks of the trapped
- Davos grows stronger
- The Shadow Plane merger expands
- Another group of heroes must be called

## Legacy

This campaign establishes:
- The threat of Taovanian artifacts
- The danger of the Shadow Plane
- The corruption possible through deals with dark entities
- The importance of Asti's Seekers in fighting such corruption

The defeat of Davos and destruction of the Devil Cube is remembered as one of the great victories against Taovan's influence, though rumors persist of other such artifacts hidden throughout Mazariim.

## Player Characters

New heroes who ventured into the cursed lands to investigate the "Spike of Asti" and became trapped, forcing them to find a way to break the curse and escape.
