# Cypher System Fantasy Conditions

## Appendix 6: Conditions

Standard conditions that can affect characters and creatures in Cypher System Fantasy.

---

## Blinded

A creature that cannot see.

- A blinded creature can't see and automatically fails any task or check that requires sight.
- Attack and Defense tasks by the creature are hindered by two steps.

---

## Charmed

A creature magically influenced to regard someone favorably.

- A charmed creature can't attack the charmer or target the charmer with harmful abilities or magical effects.
- A charmed creature's Intellect defense tasks against the charmer that involve social interactions are hindered.

---

## Dazed

A creature that is disoriented or confused.

*(Note: This condition appears in the rules overview but detailed effects should be applied by the GM based on context.)*

---

## Deafened

A creature that cannot hear.

- A deafened creature can't hear and automatically fails any task or check that requires hearing.

---

## Frightened

A creature scared by a threatening source.

- A frightened creature's skill checks and attacks are hindered while the source of its fear is within line of sight.
- The creature can't willingly move closer to the source of its fear.

---

## Grappled

A creature held or restrained by another.

- A grappled creature's speed becomes 0, and it can't benefit from any bonus to its speed.
- The condition ends if the grappler is incapacitated (see the condition).
- The condition also ends if an effect removes the grappled creature from the reach of the grappler or grappling effect, such as when a creature is hurled away by the thunder wave spell.

---

## Incapacitated

A creature that cannot take actions.

- An incapacitated creature can't take actions.

---

## Invisible

A creature that cannot be seen.

- An invisible creature is impossible to see without the aid of magic or a special sense. For the purpose of hiding, the creature is heavily obscured.
- Attack and defense tasks against an invisible creature are hindered two steps.

---

## Paralyzed

A creature frozen in place and unable to act.

- A paralyzed creature is incapacitated (see the condition) and can't move or speak.
- The creature automatically fails Might and Speed tasks.
- Attack rolls against the creature are eased two steps.
- Any attack that hits the creature is a critical hit if the attacker is within 5 feet of the creature.

---

## Petrified

A creature transformed into stone or another solid substance.

- A petrified creature is transformed, along with any object it is wearing or carrying, into a solid inanimate substance (usually stone). Its weight increases by a factor of ten, and it ceases aging.
- The creature is incapacitated (see the condition), can't move or speak, and is unaware of its surroundings.
- Attack rolls against the creature are eased two steps.
- The creature automatically fails Might and Speed tasks.
- The creature is immune to poison and disease, although a poison or disease already in its system is suspended, not neutralized.

---

## Poisoned

A creature suffering from poison.

- A poisoned creature is hindered on all tasks.

---

## Prone

A creature lying on the ground.

- A prone creature's only movement option is to crawl, unless it stands up and thereby ends the condition.
- The creature is hindered on attack rolls.
- A defense roll against the creature is hindered if the attacker is within 5 feet of the creature. Otherwise, the defense roll is eased.

---

## Restrained

A creature bound or otherwise restricted in movement.

- A restrained creature's speed becomes 0, and it can't benefit from any bonus to its speed.
- Attack and defense rolls by the creature are hindered.
- Spellcasters cannot cast spells with somatic components while restrained.
- It is impossible to attack with weapons unless they have the light property.

---

## Stunned

A creature dazed and barely able to act.

- A stunned creature is incapacitated (see the condition), can't move, and can speak only falteringly.
- The creature automatically fails Might and Speed tasks.
- Defense tasks by the creature are hindered.

---

## Unconscious

A creature that is completely unaware and inactive.

- An unconscious creature is incapacitated (see the condition), can't move or speak, and is unaware of its surroundings.
- The creature drops whatever it's holding and falls prone.
- The creature automatically fails Might and Speed tasks.
- Any attack that hits the creature is a critical hit if the attacker is within 5 feet of the creature.

---

*These conditions are standard effects that GMs can apply to characters and creatures during gameplay. Multiple conditions can affect a creature simultaneously, and their effects stack.*
