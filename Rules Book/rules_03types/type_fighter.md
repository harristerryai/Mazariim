# Fighter

**Type**: Martial Warrior
**Role**: Martial combat specialist who masters weapons, armor, and tactical combat

## Starting Stat Pools
- **Might**: 10
- **Speed**: 10
- **Intellect**: 8
- **Additional Points**: As determined by your campaign's character progression chart

## Starting Edge
Might or Speed Edge of 1 (choose one)

## Weapon and Armor Proficiencies
You are practiced in all types of weapons. You are practiced in all types of armor.

## Starting Skills
Choose three skills from the following list in which you are not already trained: Acrobatics, Animal Handling, Athletics, Craft, Intimidation, Language, Perception, Profession, Stealth, Survival, Vehicle.

## Artifact Attunement

| Level | Artifacts |
|-------|-----------|
| 1–3 | 2–3 |
| 4–6 | 3–4 |
| 7–10 | 4 |

## Low-Tier Abilities (Levels 1–3)

### Extra Attack
At level 3, you gain an additional attack on your turn. You make two attacks with the same weapon as your single action, making two separate attack rolls. These attacks do not need to be against the same target. This ability does not stack with Rapid Attack or Quick Throw.

### Martial Skills (choose per progression chart)
- **Skill with Attacks**: Choose one weapon type or weapon group (such as light bladed, heavy bludgeoning, ranged, etc.). You are trained in attacks using that weapon type.
- **Skill with Defense**: Choose one type of defense task (Might, Speed, or Intellect). You are trained in that defense.
- **Experienced in Armor**: You reduce the Speed penalty for wearing armor by 1.

### Special Abilities

**Advantage to Disadvantage** (3 Intellect points): You use trickery and deception to turn one asset that an enemy has into a hindrance for them instead. The effect lasts for one minute. Action.

**Bash** (1 Might point): This is a pummeling melee attack. Your attack inflicts 1 less point of damage than normal, but dazes your target for one round, during which time all tasks it performs are hindered. Action.

**Careful Aim**: You are trained in attacks with all weapons that you throw. Enabler.

**Combat Prowess**: You add +1 damage to one type of weapon attack of your choice: melee weapon attacks or ranged weapon attacks. Enabler.

**Combat Reflexes***: You can take more than one attack of opportunity per round, gaining an additional one for every Edge you have in your Speed pool. Enabler.

**Control the Field** (1 Might point): This melee attack inflicts 1 less point of damage than normal, and regardless of whether you hit the target, you maneuver it into a position you desire within immediate range. Action.

**Crushing Blow*** (1 Might point): When you use a bashing or bladed weapon in both hands and apply Effort on the attack, you get a free level of Effort on the damage. Enabler.

**Deadly Aim** (3 Speed points): For the next minute, all ranged attacks you make inflict 2 additional points of damage. Action to initiate.

**Dodge and Resist** (3 Speed points): You can reroll any of your Might, Speed, or Intellect defense rolls and take the better of the two results. Enabler.

**Dodge and Respond** (3 Might points): If a melee attack misses you, you can immediately make a melee attack in return, but no more than once per turn. Enabler.

**Double Strike*** (3 Might points): When you wield two weapons, you can make one attack roll. If you hit, you inflict damage with both weapons plus 2 additional points. Armor is subtracted only once. Action.

**Dual Light Wield***: You can use two light weapons at the same time without the usual penalties, making two separate attacks on your turn as a single action. Enabler.

**Everything Is a Weapon**: You can take any small object — a coin, a pen, a bottle, a stone, and so on — and throw it with such force and precision that it inflicts damage as a light weapon. Enabler.

**Fists of Fury**: You inflict 2 additional points of damage with unarmed attacks. Enabler.

**Fleet of Foot***: You can move a short distance as part of another action. You can move a long distance as your entire action for a turn. Enabler.

**Hemorrhage** (3 Might points): If you successfully attack a foe, that foe takes 3 additional points of damage at the end of the next round. Action.

**Improved Bull Rush***: Your attempts to bull rush an opponent do not provoke an attack of opportunity and your attempt is eased one step. Enabler.

**Improved Disarm***: Your attempts to disarm an opponent do not provoke an attack of opportunity, and your attempt is eased one step. Enabler.

**Improved Edge**: Choose one of your Edge stats that is 0. It becomes 1. Enabler.

**Improved Grapple***: Your attempts to grapple an opponent do not provoke an attack of opportunity, and your attempt is eased one step. Enabler.

**Improved Overrun***: Your attempts to overrun an opponent do not provoke an attack of opportunity, and your attempt is eased one step. Enabler.

**Improved Sunder***: Your attempts to sunder an opponent's weapon or item do not provoke an attack of opportunity, and your attempt is eased one step. Enabler.

**Improved Trip***: Your attempts to trip or knockdown an opponent do not provoke an attack of opportunity, and your attempt is eased one step. Enabler.

**Lunge** (2 Might points): This ability requires you to extend yourself for a powerful stab or smash. The awkward lunge hinders the attack roll. If your attack is successful, it inflicts 4 additional points of damage. Action.

**Moving Like Water** (3 Speed points): You spin and move so that your defense and movement tasks are eased for one minute. Enabler.

**No Need for Weapons**: When fighting unarmed, your attacks are eased. Enabler.

**Overwatch** (1 Intellect point): You use a ranged weapon to target a limited area (such as a doorway, a hallway, or the eastern side of the clearing) and make an attack against the next viable target to enter that area. This effect lasts for one minute. Action.

**Physical Skills***: You are trained in two skills in which you are not already trained (Acrobatics, Athletics, Intimidation, Survival). Enabler.

**Point Blank Shot***: When you make a ranged attack while threatened, you do not provoke an attack of opportunity. Enabler.

**Precise Cut**: You inflict 1 additional point of damage with light bladed weapons. Enabler.

**Precision***: You deal 1 additional point of damage with ranged attacks. Enabler.

**Quick Throw** (2 Speed points): After using a thrown light weapon, you draw another light weapon and make another thrown attack against the same target or a different one. Action.

**Rapid Reload***: Ranged weapons that take a move action to reload become so familiar, you can reload without using an action. Enabler.

**Reload** (1 Speed point): When using a weapon that normally requires an action to reload, such as a heavy crossbow, you can reload and fire (or fire and reload) in the same action. Enabler.

**Seize the Moment** (4+ Speed points): If you succeed on a Speed defense roll to resist an attack, you gain an action. You can use this action immediately even if you have already taken a turn in the round. You don't take an action during the next round, unless you apply a level of Effort when using Seize the Moment. Enabler.

**Shield Master**: When you use a shield, in addition to the asset it gives you (easing Speed defense tasks), you can act as if you are trained in Speed defense tasks. However, in any round in which you use this benefit, your attacks are hindered. Enabler.

**Slice** (2 Speed points): This is a quick attack with a bladed or pointed weapon that is hard to defend against. You are trained in this task. If the attack is successful, it deals 1 less point of damage than normal. Action.

**Spray** (2 Speed points): If a weapon has the ability to fire rapid shots without reloading, you can spray multiple shots around your target to increase the chance of hitting. This move uses 1d6 + 1 rounds of ammo (or all the ammo in the weapon, if it has less than the number rolled). The attack roll is eased. If the attack is successful, it deals 1 less point of damage than normal. Action.

**Sturdy**: You are trained in Might defense tasks. Enabler.

**Successive Attack** (2 Speed points): If you take down a foe, you can immediately make another attack on that same turn against a new foe within your reach. The second attack is part of the same action. You can use this ability with melee attacks and ranged attacks. Enabler.

**Swipe** (1 Speed point): This is a quick, agile melee attack. Your attack inflicts 1 less point of damage than normal but dazes your target for one round, during which time all tasks it performs are hindered. Action.

**Trained Without Armor**: You are trained in Speed defense tasks when not wearing armor. Enabler.

**Trick Shot** (2 Speed points): As part of the same action, you make a ranged attack against two targets that are within immediate range of each other. Make a separate attack roll against each target. The attack rolls are hindered. Action.

**Vigilance** (2 Intellect points): You take a cautious approach to combat, focusing more on protecting yourself than on hurting your opponents. While this ability is active, you gain an asset on Speed defense rolls against melee and ranged attacks, and your melee and ranged attacks are hindered. This effect lasts for as long as you wish, but it ends if no combat is taking place within range of your senses. Action to initiate.

**Weapon Crafter**: You are trained in crafting tasks associated with your chosen weapon. Enabler.

**Weapon Defense**: While your chosen weapon is in your hand(s), you are trained in Speed defense rolls. Enabler.

**Weapon Master**: You inflict an additional 1 point of damage with your chosen weapon. Enabler.

## Mid-Tier Abilities (Levels 4–6)

### Martial Skills (choose per progression chart)
- **Mastery with Attacks**: Choose one weapon type or weapon group. If you are already trained, you become specialized. If untrained, you become trained.
- **Mastery with Defense**: Choose one type of defense task (Might, Speed, or Intellect). If you are already trained, you become specialized. If untrained, you become trained.
- **Mastery in Armor**: You reduce the Speed penalty for wearing armor by an additional 1.

### Special Abilities

**Amazing Effort** (5 Might, Speed, or Intellect points): When you apply Effort, you can apply a free level of Effort on the same action. You can do this once per ten-minute rest period. Enabler.

**Capable Warrior**: Your attacks deal 1 additional point of damage. Enabler.

**Divert Attacks** (4 Speed points): For one minute, you automatically deflect or dodge any ranged projectile attacks. However, on your next turn after you're attacked with ranged projectiles, all your other actions are hindered. Action to initiate.

**Dual Defense**: When you wield two weapons, you are trained in Speed defense tasks. Enabler.

**Dual Distraction** (1+ Speed points): When you wield two weapons, your opponent's next attack is hindered, and if you apply Effort on your next attack against that same foe, you get a free level of Effort on the task. Enabler.

**Experienced Defender**: When wearing armor, you gain +1 to Armor. Enabler.

**Feint** (2 Speed points): If you use one action creating a misdirection or diversion, in the next round you can take advantage of your opponent's lowered defenses. Make a melee attack roll against that opponent. You gain an asset on this attack. If your attack is successful, it inflicts 4 additional points of damage. Action.

**Improved Success**: When you roll a natural 19 or 20 on an attack roll, you can choose to succeed automatically rather than roll. Enabler.

**Increased Effects**: You treat rolls of natural 19 or 20 as rolls of 20 when determining the effects of your attacks. This means that, on a roll of natural 19 or 20, you deal 4 additional points of damage. If you roll a natural 20 and the GM allows you to take a special action, this action can be more powerful. Enabler.

**Jump Attack** (5+ Might points): You attempt a difficulty 4 Might roll to jump high into the air as part of your melee attack action. If you succeed at the jump and your attack hits, you inflict 3 additional points of damage and knock the foe prone. If you fail at the jump, you still make your normal attack roll, but you don't deal the extra damage or knock down the opponent. For each level of Effort applied to the jump, the difficulty is decreased by one step. Enabler.

**Mobility***: You do not draw attacks of opportunity for your movement. Enabler.

**Momentum**: If you use an action to move, your next attack made using a melee weapon before the end of the next round inflicts 2 additional points of damage. Enabler.

**Never Fumble**: If you roll a natural 1 when attacking with your chosen weapon, you can ignore or countermand the complication for that roll. You can never be disarmed of your chosen weapon, nor will you ever drop it accidentally. Enabler.

**Nothing but Defend**: If you do nothing on your turn but defend, you are specialized in all defense tasks for one round. Action.

**Parry** (5 Speed points): You can deflect incoming attacks quickly. When you use this ability, you ease your Speed defense roll against that attack by one step. If you are attacked more than once in a round, the difficulty of each additional Speed defense roll is eased one further step (to a maximum of three additional steps). Enabler.

**Snipe** (2 Speed points): If you spend one action aiming, in the next round you can make a precise ranged attack. You have an asset on this attack. If your attack is successful, it inflicts 4 additional points of damage. Action.

**Stun Attack** (6 Speed points): You attempt a difficulty 5 Speed task to stun a creature as part of your melee or ranged attack. If you succeed, your attack inflicts its normal damage and stuns the creature for one round, causing it to lose its next turn. If you fail, you still make your normal attack roll, but you don't stun the opponent if you hit. Action.

**Tough As Nails**: When you are impaired or debilitated on the damage track, Might-based tasks and defense rolls you attempt are eased. Enabler.

**Tower of Will**: You are trained in Intellect defense tasks and gain +3 points to your Intellect Pool. Enabler.

**Whirlwind of Throws** (5 Speed points): With a large handful of small objects — tiny knives, shuriken, stones, jagged bits of metal, coins, or whatever is on hand — you attack every creature in an immediate area within short range. You must make attack rolls against each target. Each attack is hindered. You inflict 3 points of damage on targets you hit. Action.

## High-Tier Abilities (Levels 7–10)

### Triple Attack
At level 10, you gain a third attack on your turn, in addition to your standard attack and extra attack. You make three attacks with the same weapon as your single action, making three separate attack rolls. These attacks do not need to be against the same target.

### Martial Skills (choose per progression chart)
- **Expertise with Attacks**: Choose one weapon type or weapon group. If you are already specialized, you become expert. If trained, you become specialized. If untrained, you become trained.
- **Expertise with Defense**: Choose one type of defense task (Might, Speed, or Intellect). If you are already specialized, you become expert. If trained, you become specialized. If untrained, you become trained.

### Special Abilities

**Again and Again** (5 Speed points): Once per round, you can take an extra action on your turn. You can do this for the next minute. Action to initiate.

**Deadly Strike** (5 Might points): If you strike a foe of level 3 or lower with a weapon you're practiced with, you kill the target instantly. Action.

**Defense Master**: Every time you succeed at a Speed defense task, you can make an immediate attack against your foe. Your attack must be the same type (melee weapon, ranged weapon, or unarmed) as the attack you defend against. If you don't have an appropriate type of weapon ready, you can't use this ability. Enabler.

**Disarming Attack** (5 Speed points): You attempt a Speed task to disarm a foe as part of your melee attack. If you succeed, your attack inflicts 3 additional points of damage and the target's weapon is knocked from their grip, landing up to 20 feet (6 m) away. If you fail, you still attempt your normal attack, but you don't inflict the extra damage or disarm the opponent if you hit. Action.

**Extreme Mastery** (6 Might or 6 Speed points): When using your chosen weapon, you can reroll any attack roll you wish and take the better of the two results. Enabler.

**Finishing Blow** (5 Might points): If your foe is prone, stunned, or somehow helpless or incapacitated when you strike, you inflict 7 additional points of damage on a successful hit. Enabler.

**Lethal Damage**: Choose one of your existing attacks that inflicts points of damage (depending on your type and focus). When you hit with that attack, you inflict an additional 5 points of damage. Enabler.

**Magnificent Moment**: If you make an attack or attempt a task with the immediate action you gain by using Seize the Moment, the attack or task is eased. Enabler.

**Murderer** (8+ Speed points): With a swift and sudden attack, you strike a foe in a vital spot. If the target is level 4 or lower, it is killed outright. For each additional level of Effort you apply, you can increase the level of the target by 1. Action.

**Spin Attack** (5+ Speed points): You stand still and make attacks against up to five foes, all as part of the same action in one round. All of the attacks have to be the same sort of attack (melee or ranged). Make a separate attack roll for each foe. You remain limited by the amount of Effort you can apply on one action. Anything that modifies your attack or damage applies to all of these attacks. In addition to the normal options for using Effort, you can choose to use Effort to increase the number of foes you can attack with this ability, one additional foe per level of Effort. Action.

**Wear It Well**: When you wear armor of any kind, you gain an additional +1 to Armor. Enabler.

**Weapon and Body** (5 Speed points): After making a melee weapon or ranged weapon attack, you follow up with a punch or kick as an additional attack, all as part of the same action in one round. The two attacks can be directed at different foes. Make a separate attack roll for each attack. You remain limited by the amount of Effort you can apply on one action. Anything that modifies your attack or damage applies to both attacks, unless it's specifically tied to your weapon. Action.
