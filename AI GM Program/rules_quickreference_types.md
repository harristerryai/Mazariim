## Edge Progression

All types start with **1 Edge** in their primary stat(s):
- **Barbarian:** Might OR Speed
- **Bard:** Intellect
- **Druid:** Intellect
- **Fighter:** Might OR Speed
- **Magus:** Might, Speed, OR Intellect
- **Mage:** Intellect
- **Paladin:** Might OR Speed
- **Priest:** Intellect
- **Ranger:** Might OR Speed
- **Rogue:** Speed OR Intellect

Additional Edge can be gained through type abilities, artifacts, or flavors.

---

## Effort Progression

All types start with **Effort 1** and gain additional Effort at:
- **Tier 3:** Effort 2
- **Tier 5:** Effort 3
- **Tier 6:** Effort 4 (some types)
