## Type Abilities (Mage)

**Magical Training** (Enabler):
You are trained in the basics of magic. Your magical abilities are considered spells. You are practiced in Spellcraft. All spells require somatic components (one hand free) and verbal components (Draconic syllables). You cannot cast spells in physical armor you are not trained in using. Enabler.

**Cantrip** (1 Intellect point):
From your extensive study of magic, you have learned some minor spells called cantrips. You can cast these spells to perform minor magical effects as you choose, though the effects cannot harm or damage creatures or objects. This power includes the Light, Message, Hand, and Prestidigitation spells. Action.

**Resonance Field** (1 Intellect point):
Faint lines in a color you choose form a tracery over your entire body and emit faint light. The effect lasts for one minute. Whenever a creature within immediate range makes an attack against you, the pattern energizes to block the attack. You can make an Intellect defense roll in place of the defense roll you would normally make. If you do so and you get a minor effect, the creature attacking you takes 1 point of damage. If you get a major effect, the creature attacking you takes 4 points of damage. Action to initiate.

**Enlarge Spell** (Enabler):
You can spend a level of Effort to increase the range of a spell one step (touch to immediate, immediate to short, short to long, long to very long). Enabler.

**Extend Spell** (Enabler):
You can spend a level of Effort to change the duration of a spell from one/ten minutes to one hour, one hour to eight hours, or eight hours to twenty-four hours. Enabler.

**Battlecaster** (Enabler):
When you spend a level of Effort on a spell attack, you gain a free level on damage. Enabler.

**Identify** (Divination, 1 Int after Focus):

An invisible barrier of magical force protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you gain +2 to Armor (stacks with Mage Armor) and you take no damage from Magic Missile. Can be cast as a reaction when hit by an attack. Duration: 1 round. Action or Reaction.

## Background Notes

Vaelthrix Stormcrown is a scholar of ancient lineage, the draconic blood of blue dragons flowing through his veins. Where others see the world as it appears, Vaelthrix sees what lies beneath — the currents of magic, the threads of thought, the hidden patterns that bind reality together. His studies have led him to the school of Divination, the magic of knowing, and he has devoted himself to uncovering truths that others cannot perceive.

His draconic heritage manifests in subtle ways: faintly scaled skin along his forearms and neck that shimmers with a blue-grey iridescence, eyes that flash with electric light when he casts, and an occasional crackle of static that follows him in dry air. He carries himself with the quiet confidence of someone who has already seen how the conversation will end.

He is brilliant and deeply knowledgeable, but socially awkward — more comfortable with books than with people, more at ease deciphering an ancient cipher than making small talk at a tavern. His few social graces are the ones he has learned from books, and they show.

## Ability Choices Rationale

### Tier 1 Abilities (Cantrip + 3)

**Cantrip** — Mandatory. Provides Light, Message, Hand, Prestidigitation for minor utility.

**Enlarge Spell** — Metamagic. Allows extending range one step for 1 Effort. Critical for a backline caster who wants to stay far from danger. Extends Scorching Ray or other short-range spells to long range when needed.

**Extend Spell** — Metamagic. Allows extending duration for 1 Effort. Makes Mage Armor last much longer, See Invisibility into multi-hour duration. Essential for a prepared caster who wants buffs to last.

**Resonance Field** — Defensive. Only costs 1 Intellect (0 after Edge on a good day). Lets the wizard substitute Intellect defense for any defense roll. With Intellect Edge 2 and trained Intellect defense, this makes Vaelthrix far more survivable than a typical Mage. The minor/major effect damage feedback is a bonus.

### Tier 2 Ability

**Battlecaster** — Offensive. When spending Effort on a spell attack roll, gain free Effort on damage. This is the premier offensive enabler for spellcasters. Combined with True Strike (free divination asset) and Effort, it allows Vaelthrix to deliver devastating precision strikes despite being a diviner rather than an evoker. One Effort on attack = eased attack AND +3 damage, for only 1 Intellect point after Edge.

### Abilities Not Taken and Why

**Brew Potion** — Useful but not critical at Tier 2. Can be taken later.

**Scribe Scroll** — Same reasoning. Crafting abilities scale better at higher tiers.

**Knowledge Is Power / Knowledge Skills / Investigative Skills** — Vaelthrix already has 8 trained skills from Mage, Learned, Magical Training, and advancement. Additional skill abilities would have diminishing returns.

**Critter Companion** — Thematic for some wizards, but a level 1 familiar is fragile and Vaelthrix's concept is a lone scholar, not a beast handler.

**Monster Lore** — Useful but Vaelthrix already has extensive knowledge skills. Investigation and Knowledge: Arcana cover most monster identification situations.

**There's Your Problem** — Niche. Problem-solving is covered by Investigation and Intelligence.

## Tactical Notes

**Strengths:** Vaelthrix excels at information gathering, problem solving, and precision magical strikes. His divination spells are cheap or free thanks to Spell Focus. He has enormous Intellect reserves (21 pool, Edge 2) giving him exceptional endurance for spellcasting. Battlecaster + True Strike allows devastating combo attacks. Resonance Field gives him a strong defensive option using his best stat.

**Weaknesses:** Physically fragile (Might 10, Speed 9, no Edge in either). Cannot wear armor. Limited to light weapons for melee. Social inability from Learned descriptor hinders persuasion. No area damage spells in current spellbook (Dragonbreath covers emergencies). No escape or mobility spells prepared (consider adding Misty Step to spellbook at Tier 3).

**Combat Priority:** Cast Mage Armor at start of day (8 hour duration). In combat, activate Resonance Field first round if enemies are close. Use True Strike + Scorching Ray combo for high-value targets. Magic Missile for guaranteed damage on hard-to-hit targets. Shield as reaction when hit. Dragonbreath as emergency close-range option (once per long rest). Stay at long range whenever possible.

**Resource Management:** With Intellect Edge 2, most spells cost 0-1 points less than listed. True Strike is free. Cantrip costs 0 after Edge. One level of Effort costs only 1 point. Vaelthrix can sustain approximately 15-20 spell actions before running low on Intellect, making him exceptionally efficient for a Tier 2 caster.
