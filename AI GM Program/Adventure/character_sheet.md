## Background Notes

Vaelthrix Stormcrown is a scholar of ancient lineage, the draconic blood of blue dragons flowing through his veins. A member of the Astral Circle stationed at the Beneshire Tower south of Wyebluff, he occupies the fourth floor where his research into ley-line geometry and divination theory has progressed quietly for several years. His Castellan, Aldric Drevon, values Vaelthrix's theoretical rigor even when others dismiss his work as impractical.

His draconic heritage manifests in faintly scaled skin along his forearms and neck that shimmers with blue-grey iridescence, eyes that flash with electric light when he casts, and an occasional crackle of static in dry air. He carries himself with the quiet confidence of someone who has already seen how the conversation will end.

Brilliant and deeply knowledgeable but socially awkward, Vaelthrix is more comfortable with books than people, more at ease deciphering an ancient cipher than making small talk at a tavern. His few social graces are the ones he has learned from books, and they show.
