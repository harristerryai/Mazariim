## THE MAJOR ACCOMPLISHMENT

The expedition recovers **complete methodology** for ley-line creation:

1. **The Athenaeum** provides the Resonance Atlas—historical maps of the scar network showing where ley-lines once ran
2. **Vorthax** provides the theoretical framework explaining how ley-lines functioned and why previous recreation attempts failed
3. **The Crystalline Matrix** provides exact parameters—the geometric configurations, mana patterns, and initiation sequences encoded in its structure
4. **Sevan's gift** provides the ability to perceive scars and apply theory to physical geography
5. **Vaelith's work** provides the interpretive key that makes the matrix readable

Together, these elements constitute everything needed to eventually recreate ley-lines—though actual recreation will require resources, time, coordination, and courage far beyond this single expedition. The novel ends not with triumph complete, but with triumph *possible*—the foundation laid for work that will take years or decades to fulfill.
