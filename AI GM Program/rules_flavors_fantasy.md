# Cypher System Fantasy Flavors

Flavors are collections of abilities that can be added to any character type to simulate multiclassing or specialized training. When you choose a flavor, you gain access to abilities from that flavor's list at each tier instead of (or in addition to) abilities from your type.

---

## Combat Flavor

This flavor provides martial training and combat abilities for characters who want to blend fighting prowess with their primary type abilities.

### First Tier Combat Abilities

**Dual Light Wield**: You can use two light weapons at the same time without the usual penalties, making two separate attacks on your turn as a single action. Enabler.

**Danger Sense** (1 Speed point): The cost for using Effort on initiative tasks is reduced by one (minimum 1). Enabler.

**Practiced in Armor**: You can wear light armor without suffering its Speed penalty. Enabler.

**Practiced With Guns**: You can use guns without penalty. Enabler.

**Practiced With Medium Weapons**: You can use medium weapons without penalty. Enabler.

### Second Tier Combat Abilities

**Bloodlust** (1 Might point): After you kill a foe, on your next turn you ease all attack rolls by one step. Enabler.

**Combat Prowess**: You add +1 damage to one type of weapon attack of your choice (melee weapon attacks or ranged weapon attacks). Enabler.

**Trained Without Armor**: You are trained in Speed defense tasks when not wearing armor. Enabler.

**Practiced With Medium Armor**: You can wear medium armor without suffering its Speed penalty. You must be practiced in light armor before choosing this ability. Enabler.

### Third Tier Combat Abilities

**Fleet of Foot**: You can move a short distance as part of another action. You can move a long distance as your entire action for a turn. Enabler.

**Point Blank Shot**: When you make a ranged attack while threatened, you do not provoke an attack of opportunity. Enabler.

**Practiced With All Weapons**: You can use all weapons without penalty. Enabler.

**Practiced With Heavy Armor**: You can wear heavy armor without suffering its Speed penalty. You must be practiced in medium armor before choosing this ability. Enabler.

**Range Increase**: Ranges for you are increased by one step. Immediate becomes short, short becomes long, long becomes very long. Enabler.

**Rapid Reload**: Ranged weapons that take a move action to reload become so familiar to you that you can reload without using an action. Enabler.

**Skill With Attacks**: Choose one weapon size/type or one weapon group. You are trained in attacks using that weapon. Enabler.

**Skill With Defense**: Choose one type of defense task (Might, Speed, or Intellect). You are trained in that defense. Enabler.

**Successive Attack** (2 Speed points): If you take down a foe, you can immediately make another attack on that same turn against a new foe within your reach. The second attack is part of the same action. You can use this ability with melee attacks and ranged attacks. Enabler.

### Fourth Tier Combat Abilities

**Capable Warrior**: Your attacks deal 1 additional point of damage. Enabler.

**Deadly Aim** (3 Speed points): For the next minute, all ranged attacks you make inflict 2 additional points of damage. Action to initiate.

**Experienced in Armor**: The Speed penalty for wearing armor is reduced by 1. Enabler.

**Fury** (3 Might points): For the next minute, all melee attacks you make inflict 2 additional points of damage. Action to initiate.

**Misdirect** (3 Speed points): When an opponent misses you with a melee attack, you can redirect their attack to another target (a creature or object) of your choosing that's within immediate range of you. Make a Speed roll with a difficulty equal to the attacking creature's level. On a success, the misdirected attack hits the new target. On a failure, the attack misses completely. Enabler.

**Spray** (2 Speed points): If a weapon has the ability to fire rapid shots without reloading, you can spray multiple shots around your target to increase the chance of hitting. This uses 1d6 + 1 rounds of ammo. The attack roll is eased. If the attack is successful, it deals 1 less point of damage than normal. Action.

**Trick Shot** (2 Speed points): As part of the same action, you make a ranged attack against two targets that are within immediate range of each other. Make a separate attack roll against each target. The attack rolls are hindered. Action.

### Fifth Tier Combat Abilities

**Experienced Defender**: When wearing armor, you gain +1 to Armor. Enabler.

**Extra Attack**: You gain an additional attack on your turn. You make two attacks with the same weapon as a single action, making two separate attack rolls. These attacks do not need to be against the same target. This ability does not stack with Rapid Attack or Quick Throw. Enabler.

**Hard Target**: You are trained in Speed defense tasks when wearing light or no armor. Enabler.

**Parry** (5 Speed points): You can deflect incoming attacks quickly. When you use this ability, you ease your Speed defense roll against that attack by one step. If you are attacked more than once in a round, the difficulty of each additional Speed defense roll is eased one further step (maximum three additional steps). Enabler.

**Snipe** (2 Speed points): If you spend one action aiming, in the next round you can make a precise ranged attack. You have an asset on this attack. If your attack is successful, it inflicts 4 additional points of damage. Action.

### Sixth Tier Combat Abilities

**Mastery With Attacks**: Choose one type of attack in which you are trained. You become specialized in attacks using that type. Enabler.

**Mastery With Defense**: Choose one type of defense task in which you are trained. You become specialized in that defense. Enabler.

## Magic Flavor

This flavor provides spellcasting abilities and magical training for characters who want to blend magical prowess with their primary type abilities.

### First Tier Magic Abilities

**Cantrip** (1 Intellect point): From your study of magic, you can perform minor tricks. These tricks include the Light, Message, and Hand spells. Additionally, it includes Prestidigitation for Mages, Thaumaturgy for Priests, and Druidcraft for Druids. Action.

**Knowledge Skills**: You are trained in two skills in which you are not already trained (Appraise, Animal Handling, Language, Knowledge, Research). Enabler.

**Resonance Field** (1 Intellect point): Faint lines in a color you choose form a tracery over your entire body and emit faint light. The effect lasts for one minute. Whenever a creature within immediate range makes an attack against you, the pattern energizes to block the attack. You can make an Intellect defense roll in place of the defense roll you would normally make. If you do so and you get a minor effect, the creature attacking you takes 1 point of damage. If you get a major effect, the creature attacking you takes 4 points of damage. Action to initiate.

### Second Tier Magic Abilities

**Brew Potion**: You know how to brew potions, oils, and elixirs. Creating potions requires time, gold, and a place to work (library, shrine, or grove). The creator must meet any minimum tier requirements or material component requirements. Enabler.

**Enlarge Spell**: You can spend a level of Effort to increase the range of a spell one step (touch to immediate, immediate to short, short to long, long to very long). Enabler.

**Extend Spell**: You can spend a level of Effort to change the duration of a spell from one/ten minutes to one hour, one hour to eight hours, or eight hours to twenty-four hours. Enabler.

**Scribe Scroll**: You know how to scribe spells onto scrolls. Inscribing scrolls requires time, gold, and a place to work. Enabler.

### Third Tier Magic Abilities

**Closed Mind**: You have +3 Armor against damage that selectively targets your Intellect Pool (which normally ignores Armor). Enabler.

**Combat Casting**: Your spellcasting concentration and your defensive casting rolls are eased one step. Enabler.

**Craft Wondrous Item**: You know how to create miscellaneous magical items. Enabler.

### Fourth Tier Magic Abilities

**Spell Focus**: Choose one school of magic, one damage type, or one type of focus. You are trained in attacks with spells of that type. If the spell is not an attack, you can reduce its Intellect cost by 1 point (to a minimum of 0). Enabler.

**Spell Penetration**: When you cast a spell, you negate one level of a target's Resist Magic or similar ability. Enabler.

**Craft Arms and Armor**: You know how to craft magical weapons and magical armor. Enabler.

**Craft Wand**: You know how to craft wands that hold a limited number of charges of a specific spell. Enabler.

**Enhanced Intellect**: You gain 3 points to your Intellect Pool. Enabler.

### Fifth Tier Magic Abilities

**Augment Summoning**: When you cast a summoning spell, you can spend one level of Effort to make the summoned creature one level higher with +1 Armor and +1 Health per level. Enabler.

**Spell Power**: Choose one school of magic or one damage type. Spells of that type deal 1 additional point of damage. Enabler.

**Subtle Spell**: You can spend two levels of Effort when casting a spell to cast it without verbal or somatic components. Enabler.

**Natural Spell**: You can spend one level of Effort when casting a spell to cast it while in an altered shape (such as animal form). Enabler.

### Sixth Tier Magic Abilities

**Energy Substitution**: You can spend one level of Effort when casting a spell to change its damage type to Fire, Lightning, Cold, Acid, or Thunder. Enabler.

**Improved Counterspell**: When casting dispel magic or a similar counterspell effect, you gain a free level of Effort on the task. Enabler.

**Widen Spell**: You can spend two levels of Effort when casting a spell to double the area of effect. Enabler.

## Stealth Flavor

This flavor provides abilities focused on stealth, subterfuge, and operating unseen for characters who want to blend thief or assassin skills with their primary type.

### First Tier Stealth Abilities

**Assassin Skills**: You are trained in stealth and disguise tasks. Enabler.

**Sneak**: You are trained in stealth and initiative tasks. Enabler.

**Face Morph** (2+ Intellect points): You alter your features and coloration for one hour, hiding your identity or impersonating someone. For each level of Effort applied, you can imitate a specific person you've seen accurately enough to fool someone who knows them well. Action to initiate.

**Stealth Skills**: You are trained in two skills in which you are not already trained (Deception, Disable Device, Stealth, Sleight of Hand, Investigation). Enabler.

### Second Tier Stealth Abilities

**Contortionist** (2 Speed points): You can wriggle free from bindings or squeeze through a tight spot. You are trained in escaping. Enabler.

**Find the Way** (2+ Intellect points): When you apply Effort to a navigation task because you don't know the way, are lost, or are trying to find a shortcut, you ease the task by two steps instead of one. Enabler.

**Slip Into Shadow**: You can move a short distance and attempt a stealth task as a single action. Enabler.

**Stand Watch** (2 Intellect points): While standing watch (mostly remaining in place for an extended period of time), you unfailingly remain awake and alert for up to eight hours. During this time, you are trained in perception tasks as well as stealth tasks to conceal yourself from those who might approach. Action to initiate.

### Third Tier Stealth Abilities

**Assassin Strike** (4 Might or Speed points): If you successfully attack a creature that was previously unaware of your presence, you deal an additional 9 points of damage. Enabler.

**Blend In** (4 Intellect points): When you blend in, creatures still see you, but they attach no importance to your presence for about a minute. While blending in, you are specialized in stealth and Speed defense tasks. This effect ends if you do something to reveal your presence or position—attacking, using an ability, moving a large object, and so on. If this occurs, you can regain the remaining period of effect by taking an action to focus on seeming innocuous and as if you belong. Action to initiate or reinitiate.

**Dark Explorer**: You ignore penalties for any action (including fighting) in extremely dim light or in cramped spaces. If you also have the Eyes Adjusted ability, you can act without penalty even in total darkness. You are trained in sneaking tasks while in dim or no light. Enabler.

**Evanesce** (2 Intellect points): You step into shadows or behind cover, and everyone who was observing you completely loses track of you. Although you're not invisible, you can't be seen until you reveal yourself again by moving out of the shadows or from behind cover (or by making an attack). Action.

### Fourth Tier Stealth Abilities

**Ambusher**: When you attack a target that hasn't acted yet in combat, you ease the attack by two steps. Enabler.

**Quick Death** (2 Speed points): You know how to kill quickly. When you hit with a melee or ranged attack, you deal 4 additional points of damage. You can't use this ability more than once per round. Enabler.

**Invisibility** (4 Intellect points): You become invisible for ten minutes. While invisible, you are specialized in stealth and Speed defense tasks. This effect ends if you do something to reveal your presence or position—attacking, using an ability, moving a large object, and so on. If this occurs, you can regain the remaining invisibility effect by taking an action to focus on hiding your position. Action to initiate or reinitiate.

**Master Thief**: You are specialized in climbing, escaping from bonds, picking pockets, sleight of hand, and stealth. Enabler.

### Fifth Tier Stealth Abilities

**Assassin's Blade** (5 Speed points): When you use this ability, your next successful attack with a small bladed weapon deals an additional 6 points of damage and causes the target to immediately fall unconscious for one minute. For each level of Effort applied, you deal an additional 2 points of damage. Action to initiate.

**Thief's Luck** (3 Speed points): Reroll any rolled die that affects only you. Enabler.

**Murderer** (8+ Speed points): With a swift and sudden attack, you strike a foe in a vital spot. If the target is level 4 or lower, it is killed outright. For each additional level of Effort you apply, you can increase the level of the target by 1. Action.

**Ghost** (4 Intellect points): For the next ten minutes, you gain an asset to hiding and stealth tasks. During this time, you can move through solid barriers (even those that are sealed to prevent the passage of light or shadow), but not energy barriers, at a rate of 1 foot (30 cm) per round. You can perceive while passing through a barrier or object, which allows you to peek through walls. Action to initiate.

### Sixth Tier Stealth Abilities

**Burst of Escape** (5 Speed points): You can take two separate actions this round to attempt to hide and move. Enabler.

**Disguise Other** (5 Intellect points): You cause one creature to look and sound like someone else for up to one hour. The disguised creature must remain within immediate range the entire time. Once the creature is disguised, it can move freely within long range. If it moves beyond long range or you use this ability again, the disguise ends. Action to initiate.

**Immovable**: You gain +5 to Armor against any kind of forced movement. Enabler.

**Spring Away** (5 Speed points): When attacked, you leap up to a short distance away from your attacker as part of your defense task, potentially moving out of reach or behind cover. Enabler.

## Skills and Knowledge Flavor

This flavor provides abilities focused on knowledge, training, and specialized skills for characters who want to be experts, scholars, or jacks-of-all-trades.

### First Tier Skills and Knowledge Abilities

**Extra Skill**: You are trained in a skill of your choice (other than attacks or defense) in which you are not already trained. Enabler.

**Intelligent**: You are trained in all Intellect defense tasks. Enabler.

**Knowledge Skills**: You are trained in two skills in which you are not already trained (Appraise, Animal Handling, Language, Knowledge, Research). Enabler.

**Investigator**: You are trained in perception and investigation tasks. Enabler.

### Second Tier Skills and Knowledge Abilities

**Investigative Skills**: You are trained in two skills in which you are not already trained (Insight, Investigation, Navigation, Perception, Survival). Enabler.

**Quick Study**: You learn from repetitive actions. You gain a +1 bonus to rolls for similar tasks after the first time (such as operating the same device, picking the same kind of lock, or disabling the same type of trap). Once you move on and do other things—usually after the passage of at least a few hours—you lose the familiarity with the task. Enabler.

**Skill With Defense**: Choose one type of defense task (Might, Speed, or Intellect) in which you are not already trained. You are trained in that defense. Enabler.

**Travel Skills**: You are trained in two skills in which you are not already trained (Navigation, Survival, Swimming, Climbing, Running, or Jumping). Enabler.

### Third Tier Skills and Knowledge Abilities

**Expert**: Choose one skill in which you are trained. You become specialized in that skill. Enabler.

**Flex Skill**: At the beginning of each day, choose one task that you will ease by one step for that day (other than attacks or defense). Enabler.

**Improvise** (3 Intellect points): When you perform a task that you have no skill training in, you can improvise to gain an asset on the task. The asset might be an object, a bauble, or a straightforward lie. For example, if you need to climb a steep incline, you might improvise using a crowbar you're carrying as a climbing aid. If you want to bluff your way out of a situation, you can improvise by claiming to be someone you're not. You can't use this ability if you're trained in the task. Action.

**Skill With Attacks**: Choose one type of attack in which you are not already trained. You are trained in attacks using that type. Enabler.

### Fourth Tier Skills and Knowledge Abilities

**Capable Warrior**: Your attacks deal 1 additional point of damage. Enabler.

**Expert**: Choose a different skill in which you are trained. You become specialized in that skill. Enabler.

**Increased Effects**: You treat rolls of natural 19 or 20 as rolls of 20 when making attacks or defense rolls. This means that, on a roll of natural 19 or 20, you get a minor or major effect instead of just success. Enabler.

**Read the Signs** (4 Intellect points): You examine an area and learn precise, useful details about the past (if any exist). You can ask the GM up to four questions about the immediate area; each requires its own roll. Action.

### Fifth Tier Skills and Knowledge Abilities

**Adroit Cypher Use**: You can bear three cyphers at a time. Enabler.

**Mastery With Attacks**: Choose one type of attack in which you are trained. You become specialized in attacks using that type. Enabler.

**Mastery With Defense**: Choose one type of defense task in which you are trained. You become specialized in that defense. Enabler.

**Skill Training**: You are trained in two skills in which you are not already trained. Choose from the following: Acrobatics, Animal Handling, Athletics, Craft, Deception, Heal, Insight, Intimidation, Investigation, Knowledge, Language, Navigation, Perception, Performance, Persuasion, Profession, Research, Sleight of Hand, Spellcraft, Stealth, Survival, or Vehicle. Enabler.

### Sixth Tier Skills and Knowledge Abilities

**Feat of Strength** (4 Might points): Any task that depends on brute force is eased by two steps. Examples include smashing down a door, tearing open a locked container, lifting a heavy object, or striking someone with a melee weapon. Enabler.

**Greater Skill With Defense**: Choose one type of defense task in which you are trained. You gain an additional asset in that defense. Enabler.

**Inventor**: You can create new artifacts in half the normal time, as if you have an additional specialization in crafting. When you create an artifact, there is a chance that you discover a related ability that you can take in place of another ability. Enabler.

**Mastery in Armor**: Choose one type of armor in which you are already practiced. You gain +1 Armor while wearing that type of armor. Enabler.

## Using Flavors

When you add a flavor to your character:

1. **Choose abilities from the flavor list** instead of (or in addition to) your type's ability list at each tier
2. **Flavors don't replace your type**, they supplement it
3. **You can only have one flavor** at a time
4. **Abilities marked with an asterisk (\*)** are custom fantasy abilities described in the Cypher System Fantasy Ancestries & Types PDF or in character type files
5. **Numbers in parentheses** refer to page numbers in the Cypher System Rulebook (CSR)
