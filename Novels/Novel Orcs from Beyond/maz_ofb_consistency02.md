## 1. MAGIC SYSTEM — CORE RULES

### Mana

Magic is a fundamental force of the universe alongside gravity and
electromagnetism. Its particles are called mana. A diffuse layer
covers the world and all higher dimensions. Spellcasters manipulate
this mana through arcane formulas, innate understanding, divine
incantations, or druidic prayers.

**Antimagic:** Pushes mana particles away, removing power source.

**Inner Mana Sources:** Golems, undead, elementals, outsiders,
magical artifacts have tiny inner mana concentrations.

**Powerful Creatures:** Dragons, the Favored, entity avatars have
larger internal mana reserves.

### Magical Writings

All spells have written roots in Draconic (dragons taught first
arcanists). Arcane writings always in Draconic. Divine spells
combine faith-specific script with Draconic (e.g., Celestial +
Draconic for Tiovin; Elda'lam + Draconic for Minerva).

### Entity Rules

Entities are timeless beings formed from clumping magical energies.
They can be destroyed. When entering the prime material plane, they
must send avatars. If an avatar is destroyed, the entity needs 101
years to send another. Entities from other universes are classified
as lesser entities regardless of original power.

### Alternate Material Planes

Infinite alternates exist, each vibrating at slightly different
frequency, occupying same space. Each has its own extra-dimensional
planes (except the Astral Plane, which is shared). Only entities
form differently in each reality.

### Key Rules for Ta'Puh Scenes

**Minerva does not exist in Ta'Puh.** Aizune's divine connection
goes completely silent when they cross. Not attenuated; severed.
This affects his healing, divine senses, and illumination
capabilities. Established in Book One Ch. 10 rooftop conversation;
confirmed in Book Two Ch. 3 (arrival). **RESTORED upon return to
Mazariim (B2 Ch. 6).** The experience of loss has deepened the
connection; not returned to baseline but enhanced by contrast.

**Sensorium detection:** Twisted elves linked in psionic hivemind
detect mana signatures. If they scan heroes and find arcane or
divine resonance that does not match Orceri patterns, discovery
occurs. Established Book One Ch. 10.

**Mana saturation camouflage theory:** Smiggles theorised ambient
mana noise of Orceri cities may mask individual signatures from
sensoriums. **TESTED AND CONFIRMED (B2 Ch. 4).** Mana engine in
Aleron's pack masked signatures; sensoriums passed without pausing.

**Polymorph disguise:** Smiggles leads initial shaping (arcane
precision for physiology: skin texture, bone density, scent). Lai
layers draconic working on top (different mana source reinforces;
"lacquer over wood"). Combined duration "adequate." Draconic layer
holds longer than arcane layer beneath. Degradation signs: tusks
soften, skin ripples at knuckles, original colouring surfaces
through grey-green. Tusks itch when degradation begins (Lai's
warning, B2 Ch. 3; confirmed on Iskar, B2 Ch. 4). Established
Book One Ch. 10; executed Book Two Ch. 3.

## 2. SPELLCASTING DESCRIPTION RULES (PROSE CONVENTIONS)

### Core Rule

No spell names in prose. Characters refer to effects, not spell
names. Spells described through sensory details only.

No game mechanics terms in prose (class levels, stats, spell lists).

### How Spellcasting Appears in Prose

**Verbal Component:** Described as Draconic words/syllables.
Syllable count communicates power tier (see Section 4). Divine
casters may use fewer syllables (entity supplements verbal
component).

**Somatic Component:** Gesture patterns vary by tradition. Arcane:
precise hand geometries. Divine: focused through holy symbol or
prayer gesture. Bardic: through performance and voice.

**Draconic Casting (Lyanorra):** Verbal ONLY. No somatic or
material. Immune to silence effects. Counterspell as swift action.

**Sensory Output:** Every spell produces tradition-specific sensory
signatures (colour, scent, sound, temperature, environmental
effect). See Section 3 for established signatures.

### Power Tier Communication

Spell power communicated through:
- Syllable count of incantation
- Intensity of sensory output
- Physical toll on caster
- Environmental scale of effect

Never through numerical values, level references, or mechanical
terminology.

#### Personality and Voice

  intimate directness as natural modes of communication. Touches

  intimacy that exceeds the professional.
- **Amused by formality:** Finds mortal propriety faintly
  entertaining. Her smile when using precise academic language
  suggests she finds the contrast between content and delivery
  privately enjoyable.

**Voice characteristics:**

- Heat in speech is literal rather than figurative
- Slow confidence; never raises voice

## 12. WRITING CONVENTIONS AND STANDING RULES (Consolidated)

### Prose Rules

- No spell names in prose; describe effects through sensory detail
- No game mechanics terms (class levels, stats, spell lists)
- No em dashes or en dashes (use commas, periods, colons,
  semicolons, or restructured clauses)
- No contractions in narrative voice
- Spirit-touched elven curved blade: ALWAYS "spirit-touched" in
  prose (Aizune's weapon)
- Pala'dinae: NEVER "Paladine" — always Pala'dinae
- Ariana: NEVER "Ariano" — always Ariana Ignescreo
- Italicised text = Aizune's journal entries or internal key
  observations

### Sentence Distribution (Per 1,000 Words)

| Type | Distribution | Word Count |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Simple Clarity | 30% | 8-15 words |
| Elevated Epic | 40% | 16-30 words |
| Atmospheric Dense | 30% | 31-50 words |

Dense sentences NEVER exceed 60 words.
